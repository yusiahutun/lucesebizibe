The Comfy Birkenstock Sandals Worn by All of Hollywood Are on Sale

October 2024 · 3 minute read

One of the biggest fashion upgrades I’ve ever made was swapping out my $1 flip-flops for Birkenstock Arizona sandals, which have been worn by practically all of Hollywood at this point. Cameron Diaz, Reese Witherspoon, Tracee Ellis Ross, and Margot Robbie, are all fans, just to name a few. Not only do they look better than a dingy flip-flop, but they feel better, too; my straps no longer break mid-walk and sandal-induced foot pain has all but disappeared. And right now, a number of Arizona styles are on rare sale at Gilt.

In case you’re not familiar with Gilt, it’s the place to grab luxury items for less — all you need to do to access these rare deals is sign up for a free account. Often you’ll find discounts on brands including Ugg, Stuart Weitzman, and even Tory Burch, and this week, a number of Birkenstocks are majorly marked down.

During the retailer’s Birkenstock spring sale, you can save up to 32 percent on best-selling sandals from the brand Katie Holmes and Cindy Crawford wear. Find the eight Arizona styles we’re grabbing from the sale, below.

Arizona Birko-Flor Narrow Sandal

This scarlet pair of Arizona sandals are the most discounted at 32 percent off. This pair features a narrow fit, leather straps, a silver buckle, and a cork footbed that shapes to your foot. And though the brand might be most popular for its neutral styles, I honestly might love this colorful version the most. Red is trending this year, and a small pop of the color via your shoes is one of the easiest ways to incorporate it into your existing wardrobe.

Arizona Birko-Flor Nubuck Sandal

But if you do prefer that classic Birkenstock look, this pair of brown Arizonas is a must-buy. The neutral style — which features leather-like straps (Birko-Flor is the brand’s synthetic leather material) and that same adjusting cork footbed — is sure to go with everything in your closet and boasts hundreds of five-star reviews across retailers online. Shoppers say the sandal “fits like a glove” and that they are “ gorgeous” and “go with anything” on top of being comfortable. Someone else called even the full-priced ones “well worth the money.” 

Arizona Soft Footbed Leather Sandal

You could also make the upgrade to real leather (still for less!) with these lilac Arizonas. The color is ideal for summer and spring and the durable straps will allow you to wear them for years to come. Plus, as Robbie proved, pastel Birkenstocks look remarkably chic when worn with a pair of light wash jeans; they’re a combination perfect for warmer months.

Birkenstock’s Arizona sandals rarely go on sale, but right now, you can grab them for up to 32 percent off at Gilt. Shop more of the celebrity-loved style, below.
