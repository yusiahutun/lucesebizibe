Best 5 Benefits of Mobile Payments

June 2024 · 3 minute read

If you want to improve and grow your business, this means adapting to technology, whichever industry you work in. Using technology for payments that are mostly Mobile Payments can help you be more productive, simplify the way you deal with finances, and so much more.

Here are some of the benefits of mobile payments to your business.

1. Lower costs

Card readers are affordable and can save you money compared to other point of sale equipment. Mobile SDK integrations available here make it possible to accept a variety of payment methods from your customers. Because the payment data passes through a dedicated payment hub, you don’t have to spend more money on keeping this secure. The pre-certified software protects your customers’ payment methods for you.

2. Boost revenue

One of the Benefits of Mobile Payments is Boost revenue. The easier you make it for your customers to purchase from you, the higher the chances are of them making one or more purchases. For example, if you work in a restaurant and one of your customers hasn’t brought enough cash to cover another drink, they won’t order anything else and will settle their existing bill instead. If they can pay using their phone in just a few seconds, they might order that extra drink (or several).

3. Adapt your marketing

One of the Benefits of Mobile Payments is you can adapt marketing. If you take payments through a custom mobile app, you can use the information stored in the app to reward regular customers with coupons, special offers and loyalty points, etc. This can encourage them to return, use their offers, or save points towards a bigger reward.

It can also show you the most popular products and busier times of the day (when most payments are made). You could use this information to promote these products and find ways to encourage more customers to visit during quieter times. This could include reduced-price food or drinks during these times, or organizing events.

4. Retaining customers

Another one of the Benefits of Mobile Payments is retaining customers. Customers might choose you over a competitor if your business offers more ways to pay mostly mobile payments. For example, some places such as bars and restaurants only take card payments, while others only take cash. If you give your customers a choice, and your business is on a similar level to these other bars and restaurants, they will appreciate the choice. It could be that a group of friends decides to go out, but want to pay in different ways. If you accept all of their payment methods, this works in your favour. Customers prefer choice.

5. Make bookkeeping easier

One of the Benefits of Mobile Payments is, that your Bookkeeping will be easier. Having all your electronic payment records stored together can make it easier when you do your bookkeeping. Instead of having to locate records for different card payments and then details of other ways customers have paid, these are all in one place. So, you will find it easier to do your own accounting, or at gather all the information you need for your accountant.

Mobile payments make running a business easier. By giving your customer a choice, they are more likely to return. They also offer a way for customers to pay securely, so they can enjoy their experience worry-free.
