Who Is Lisa Parigi? Lionel Richie's Girlfriend: Everything We Know

July 2024 · 2 minute read

Is there anything better than going on a shopping spree with your beloved beau? That’s exactly what Lionel Richie and Lisa Parigi, the music icon’s much younger girlfriend, did while spending the day together on Wednesday, April 24.

The couple stopped at a few stores in Beverly Hills and it seems as though they bought everything their hearts desired. Like a true gentleman, Lionel carried all of the heavy shopping bags. The only thing Lisa had to be concerned about was where to go next!

The couple were spotted spending some quality time together after the 69-year-old “Hello” singer recently gushed to People about loving Lisa. “She’s extremely beautiful, but most importantly she’s smart and helps me deal with my crazy world,” he said. “At this time in my life when you can be so jaded about things, Lisa kind of makes everything feel a bit safe.”

The pair have been getting along so well that they have even talked about having a kid together. “I’m willing to become a father again,” Lionel, already a dad to three, told The Mirror in a 2016 interview. “I might have just let a secret slip there, but so be it — Lisa and I are cool with the idea of children. Marriage, on the other hand, is a very different issue, it’s a bad idea for men.”

Since Lionel experienced two failed marriages — to Brenda Harvey-Richie and Diane Alexander — we understand why he must be a little bit hesitant to tie the knot again. ‘I’ve been there twice and it was very expensive for me,” he admitted. “I’ve no idea to go there a third time — women have killed it for me. Kids aren’t the problem — ex-wives are. That’s why I won’t marry again, but I’m happy to commit in other ways.”

Looks like we won’t see Lisa and Lionel walk down the wedding aisle anytime soon. Down the shopping aisle, though? That’s more like it!
