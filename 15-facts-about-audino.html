Audino is known as the Hearing Pokémon.

Audino is a Normal-type Pokémon that is primarily known for its exceptional hearing abilities. Its large, rabbit-like ears serve as highly sensitive receivers that allow it to detect even the faintest of sounds.

It has a gentle and caring nature.

Audino is known for its nurturing instincts and often acts as a caretaker for other Pokémon, especially those that are injured or ill. Its presence brings comfort and healing to other creatures.

Audino has the ability to heal wounds.

Using its feelers, Audino can detect energy imbalances in other Pokémon and use its powers to restore their health. It emits soothing waves of energy that promote healing and well-being.

Audino’s Mega Evolution increases its power and defense.

When Audino undergoes Mega Evolution, it becomes even more formidable. Its hearing capabilities are enhanced, allowing it to perceive the faintest of sounds from miles away. Its healing powers are also amplified, making it an even more effective healer in battles.

Audino’s appearance resembles a nurse or medical professional.

With its pink and cream-colored body, Audino bears a resemblance to a nurse or caregiver. Its demeanor and nurturing nature further reinforce this association.

Audino can learn a variety of supportive moves.

Being a support-oriented Pokémon, Audino can learn moves like Heal Pulse, Refresh, and Wish, which are all focused on healing and supporting its teammates in battles.

Audino is a sought-after Pokémon for trainers in need of healing.

Trainers often seek out Audino not only for its battling abilities but also for its healing powers. Its presence in a team can be invaluable during difficult battles and long journeys.

Audino is found in peaceful environments.

Audino is known to inhabit calm and serene areas such as meadows and gardens. Its preference for peaceful surroundings is reflected in its gentle and calm demeanor.

Audino has a basic move called “Pound”.

Pound is one of the first moves Audino learns, and it involves delivering a straightforward, physical strike to its opponent. While not particularly powerful, it can still deal some damage.

Audino’s base experience points are 390.

When defeated in battle, Audino yields 390 base experience points. This can be beneficial for trainers looking to level up their Pokémon quickly.

Audino’s appearance resembles a rabbit and a teddy bear.

With its long ears and cute, round body, Audino has features reminiscent of both a rabbit and a teddy bear. Its adorable appearance adds to its charm and popularity.

Audino can be found in various Pokémon games.

Audino has appeared in multiple Pokémon games, including Pokémon Black and White, Pokémon X and Y, and Pokémon Sun and Moon. Trainers can encounter and capture Audino in various locations throughout their journeys.

Audino can emit ultrasonic waves.

Audino can emit ultrasonic waves from its ears, which are used both for communication with other Pokémon and for detecting its surroundings. These waves are beyond the hearing range of humans.

Audino has a unique Mega Evolution stone called the “Audinite”.

To unlock Audino’s Mega Evolution, trainers must obtain the Audinite, a special Mega Stone that triggers its transformation. This stone harnesses the power of Mega Evolution and allows Audino to reach its full potential.

Audino’s ability “Regenerator” allows it to regain health after switching out.

Audino possesses the ability “Regenerator,” which enables it to recover a portion of its HP whenever it is switched out of battle. This ability can be highly advantageous in prolonged battles.
