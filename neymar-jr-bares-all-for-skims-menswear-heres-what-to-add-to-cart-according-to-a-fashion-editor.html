Neymar Jr. bares all for Skims menswear: Here's what to add to cart according to a fashion editor

April 2024 · 2 minute read

Our favourite American shapewear brand Skims boldly makes its first foray into menswear, just in time for the holiday gifting season.

As far as celebrity-founded companies go, it's safe to say that Kim Kardashian hit the relevancy jackpot when she launched Skims back in 2016. Quickly monopolising on an industry that was basically forgotten, redefining the way shapewear is seen, made and used.

Now, seven years on from its initial inception, Skims has finally launched into menswear, launching three collections made from beloved Skims fabric genres- cotton, stretch and sport.

It wouldn't be a new Skims collab without a star-studded cast of famed faces, would it? From former Victoria's Secret Angels, Alessandra Ambrosio, Candice Swanepoel, Heidi Klum and Tyra Banks to their most recent ambassador Cardi B, the Skims family is as elite as it gets and this launch is no different.

Famed football star Neymar Jr. joins NBA player Shai Gilgeous-Alexander and San Francisco 49ers player Nick Bosa to front the launch, baring all in several different underwear silhouettes, track pants, t-shirts, tanks and leggings. 

If you’re on the hunt for a little something special for your someone special or just simply want an excuse to peruse three athletes in their knickers, consult our favourite pieces and how to wear them below. You can thank us later…

Men's 5" Boxer Brief 3-Pack

Made from cotton, spandex and modal, these boxer briefs are as comfy as they get. Top tip for the gals out there - these make for the ultimate bedtime shorts.

Men's T-Shirt 3-Pack

Time and time again a boxy men’s t-shirt has proved itself as a wardrobe staple and this white crewneck is the perfect addition. We love how it comes in a three-pack, one for gifting and two for you.

Mens Tank 3-Pack

Again, comfort is key and these ribbed tanks are nothing but. Made from 92% cotton and 8% spandex, these tanks are designed to stretch and hug all the right places, perfect for wearing under jumpers when it’s cold, or to lounge with the aforementioned boxer shorts.
