Tracey Edmonds is an Los Angeles native

Tracey Edmonds was born in February 1967 in Los Angeles, California, to her parents George and Jacqueline McQuarn.

She’s a successful entrepreneur in Hollywood

According to People, Edmonds has over 25 years experience in the TV and film industry. She runs her own production company, Edmonds Entertainment Group, where she currently serves as the CEO. The entrepreneur used to work as a TV journalist and co-hosted ExtraTV from 2014 to 2017 alongside Mario Lopez and Charissa Thompson, winning a Daytime Emmy for her part on the show to boot, per People.

She’s produced films including New in Town, End of the Road and The Postcard Killings, as seen on her IMDB.

Per her LinkedIn, the Stanford graduate is also the founder and president of AlrightNow/AlrightTV. Her IMDB profile also states that she served on the Board of Governors for the Producers Guild of America (PGA) and on the Board of Trustees for the American Film Institute.

She got engaged to Deion Sanders in 2019

Edmonds got engaged to the two-time Super Bowl champion in February 2019, per People.

In a celebratory post on Instagram, Edmonds shared her excitement: “God is so good! We’re eight years in, made it through the storms together, and will be spending the rest of our lives together!”

She added, “To all the couples out there … stay real with each other, listen to each other, and most of all … keep love and God in your heart. When you do, you can make it through any storm! Spread love and joy on this day!”. The former couple ended their relationship amicably, based on her most recent post announcing their split.

She has two children

Edmonds is a proud mama to her two sons, Brandon and Dylan, whom she shares with her ex-husband Kenneth “Babyface” Edmonds. Per People, she shares a close relationship with both of her children and has worked hard to “[raise] my kids the same way that my parents raised me” and to “keep them out of the Hollywood craziness”.

“As a single mum, I’ve always felt that it was so important to have honest and daily conversations about life with both of my boys. It’s just been the three of us inside my household, so they’ve grown up very close to me in every aspect of their lives,” she once told Tinseltown Mom.

Who has Tracey Edmonds dated?

Edmonds was married to Babyface from 1992 to 2005, before the couple called it quits. She then tied the knot for the second time with Hollywood funnyman Eddie Murphy in 2008, but the marriage was short lived as they announced they were going their separate ways less than two weeks later, People reported.

In a joint statement announcing their split, Edmonds and Murphy said, “it is not necessary to define our relationship further”.
