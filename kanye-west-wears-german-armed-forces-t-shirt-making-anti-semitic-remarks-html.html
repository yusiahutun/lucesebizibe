Kanye West wears German armed forces T-shirt after making anti-Semitic remarks

April 2024 · 6 minute read

Kanye West wore a German armed forces t-shirt as he went shopping with new wife Bianca Censori at under-fire Balenciaga in LA on Wednesday.

The disgraced rapper, 45, who saw his career and net worth go in free-fall after a series of anti-Semitic remarks, wore a T-shirt emblazoned with a vintage logo of the Bundeswehr -  the armed forces of the Federal Republic of Germany.

The logo was previously used for the German military in the post Second Word War era -  following the Holocaust in which six million Jewish people were murdered by the Nazi party, under the direction of Adolf Hitler.

The logo features the Bundesadler (Federal Eagle) -which has been used in the German coat of arms since 1945.
