Man Jumps Over Bench to Attack Judge During His Sentencing in Horrific Video

April 2024 · 2 minute read
  • A three-time felon attacked a judge in a Las Vegas courtroom after she denied him probation
  • The judge was taken to the hospital with minor injuries, while the attacker faced more charges
  • The shocking video of the attack went viral, sparking outrage and support for the judge

A shocking video has emerged of a man attacking a judge in a Las Vegas courtroom after she denied him probation.

The man, identified as Deobra Redden, a three-time felon, jumped over the bench and hit the judge, causing a chaotic scene.

Why did felon attack judge?

At his sentencing hearing, he climbed over the bench and attacked the judge when she denied him probation, as seen in the video.

Judge Mary Kay Holthus was sentencing 30-year-old Deobra Redden in Clark County District Court following his conviction for attempted violence causing significant bodily harm, according to KLAS.

Redden's lawyer requested that Holthus sentence his client to probation at around eleven, but Holthus had other plans for the offender.

Video by the Shade Rooms showed Redden running up to the bench, throwing himself over with his arms spread, and tackling the judge.

A state flag for Nevada and an American flag crashed in the chaos. In the turmoil that preceded, Redden yelled obscenities at Holthus, and the clip showed him wrestling with several other people.

While the judge was still on the ground, court officers hauled him away, but he managed to escape and attempted to follow her again, as seen on camera. He even traded blows with other participants in the fight.
