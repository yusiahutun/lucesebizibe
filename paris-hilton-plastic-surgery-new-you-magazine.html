Paris Hilton Talks Plastic Surgery -- Has She Ever Gone Under the Knife?!

September 2024 · 2 minute read

Paris Hilton says she's considered plastic surgery ... but has she ever followed through with it?!

"I've never done any [plastic surgery] in my life," she says in the latest issue of New You magazine (via People). "People love making things up, but I am very lucky that I listened to my mom through my young age. She told me to stay away from the sun, so my friends would be frying in the sun every day and now they all look much older and all have Botox."

While she admits that she approached her doctor about getting some tweaks, he wouldn't do it. "I even asked my dermatologist if I should do [plastic surgery], and he's like, 'I refuse to do it to you … Your skin is so perfect. I will not do it to you until you need it, and I don't know if you ever will,'" she explains.

"I am happy with that. I have nothing against it; all my friends have done everything — boobs, nose, chin, cheeks," she continues, without naming names. "I live in Hollywood, so I'm used to seeing it. It's just that I've never wanted to do it."

While the beautiful business mogul now has "17 different product lines and we are about to open 200 more stores," she is ready to settle down ... one day.

"I would love to get married one day. I think that's what every woman wants in life — to find someone, a partner, to be with forever and have a family," she tells the mag. "I would love to have a son and then a daughter and then either a son or daughter, because I always wished I had an older brother to watch over me and look after me. So I would love to have a boy first to watch after my little girl when I'm not going to be there, when they're at school."

Maybe there are wedding bells in the future with current beau Thomas Gross? We'll have to wait and see!
