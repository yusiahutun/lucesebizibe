Model and socialite Grace Teal has spoken openly about her secret weapon that has caused her sex drive to explode.

The 31-year-old says she started taking the substance, supplied by a UK company called CBD Armour, a year ago in a bid to combat her anxiety.

She explains: "CBD oil droplets have helped ease my feelings of anxiousness.

“I”m also much calmer in difficult situations that I might have been stressed-out in before.

“And, as an added bonus, it’s also sent my sexual appetite through the roof!”

And it’s perfectly legal to buy and consume, as its sold as a simple food supplement, and can even by used as a sexual lubricant in some instances.

"The balm is great and really helps with that.”

Grace adds: "Life is good, I run my own business as well as model and my son is growing up into a fantastic, charming little gentlemen. Life couldn't be better."
