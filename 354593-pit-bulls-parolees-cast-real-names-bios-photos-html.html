Pit Bulls and Parolees cast: meet the Torreses, Jake Gardner, and others

February 2024 · 9 minute read

The Pit Bulls and Parolees cast inspire many to love animals. The show featured Tia Torres, her family, and some parolees rescuing pit bulls from the streets. They shared the Villalobos Rescue Center (VRC) operations, from dog rescue missions, daily care & training to adoption stories.

Pit Bulls and Parolees aired on Animal Planet from October 30, 2009, to November 22, 2022. This American reality television series had 19 seasons and 203 episodes. The cast helped many understand the importance of rescuing vulnerable animals, how to rehabilitate stray dogs of different species, and the legal process of adopting pets in the US.

The Pitbulls and Parolees cast members

The Pit Bulls and Parolees cast shared with the public ways humans abuse and misjudge animals, specifically the pit bulls. They also sensitized the masses about criminal acts people commit against pets and animals in general. This article unveils the cast members of Pitbulls and Parolees, and where are they now.
