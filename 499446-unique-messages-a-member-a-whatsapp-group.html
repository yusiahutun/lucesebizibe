The welcome message examples listed here will help you welcome them and briefly describe the group.

Short welcome message

When new members join a WhatsApp group, it is an added advantage to the group's growth and increases the spread of its motive. However, if they feel unrecognised, they may be hesitant to stay. That's why appreciating their presence with a short welcome message is essential, as it makes them feel comfortable and special.

  • Here's a warm welcome to our support group. Glad you decided to join us.
  • Welcome to our group designed to share helpful stuff. We will appreciate your good stuff as we help improve our academic ability.
  • We value your presence and ideas. Welcome to the team. Looking forward to your contributions.
  • Hey there, welcome to our committee group. Let's get chatting for good wedding planning.
  • Excited to have you on our team. This is the employees' group, so feel free to get to know your colleagues.
  • It is a pleasure to welcome you to the team; we hope we all benefit as we share our experiences.
  • We are proud to have you in our group. Your leadership and positive thinking will be beneficial to us as your students.
  • Thank you for considering our group worthy of joining. We hope to spread more good deeds with you for the betterment of humanity.
  • It is a pleasure that we welcome a great teacher like you to our educational group. Your useful words will be beneficial.
  • We are pleased that you considered our group invite link. We feel privileged to have such a great motivational speaker amongst us.
  • It's an honour to have you join us. We love positivity and enthusiasm in our colleagues' group. We are eager to learn more about you.
  • Welcome to the family. Our entire relatives can't wait to meet you and officially welcome you home, and we expect unconditional love from you.
  • You're an excellent addition to our group with your level of experience and skills. We hope to learn a lot from you. Welcome to the company.

Funny welcome message in a WhatsApp group

Sending funny welcome texts paves the way for boosting new members' engagement and participation in the group.

  • Welcome aboard. Sorry, we don't have life Jackets, but we make up for it with plenty of jokes to keep you afloat.
  • Welcome to the group, and feel free to share your thoughts. We are like a support group for people addicted to sharing memes.
  • Thanks for joining our group. You have immediately been contractually obligated to laugh at most corny puns and jokes.
  • Congratulations on officially unlocking the secret entrance to the best business chat group on the internet.
  • Welcome to the family group. We hope you brought a sense of humour with you because that's exactly what we offer.
  • Welcome to the squad. The group appreciates your presence, and don't worry about your weird browsing history. We won't judge.
  • Thank you for joining our employer team. We promise to make your phone buzz with CVs; you'll think you are so popular.
  • You are now officially a group member. We are like a rollercoaster ride, but share more memes and jokes with no safety warnings here. So be ready for a rollercoaster of laughter.
  • Cheers to the newest member. Here's a glass of jokes and puns. Welcome to the group of funny memes.
  • Congratulations. You have joined a friend group, and your job will be sharing and laughing at hilarious memes and memories.

By choosing the right words with some love and a little humour, they will truly feel part of your family and not as if they are going through the motions.

  • Here it's all about good vibes and love, and as the newest family member, be prepared to be loved and heard.
  • Learning about each other is a fun adventure, and life is an adventure that our family takes together. Welcome home. Let's grow together.
  • Welcome to the family. We are a team, and the one-word family rule is- Participation.
  • Our family may lack finesse, but we make up for it by sharing and spreading unconditional love to all, so feel loved and welcome here.
  • Congratulations to new couples. Your life just became easier because you joined the group and a team of supporters from our entire family.
  • Welcome to the Army of Supporters family group. Here you will never be alone because someone will always be available.
