Short Description Of Austin Mahone

Austin Mahone is a famous singer and social media star who rose to fame through his viral videos. His performance led him to be famous as the second coming of Justin Beiber.

Moreover, he has more than 10 million followers on his Instagram and Twitter. Currently, he is signed to Universal Music Japan, BMG, AM Music LLC, and Mr. 305 Inc.

Austin Mahone’s Early Life And Education

Austin Harris Mahone aka Austin Mahone was born on April 4, 1996, in San Antonio, Texas to Charles Edgar Mahone and Michele Lee. When he was just one and a half year old, his father died. After the death of his father, he was raised by a single mother.

Mahone is of German, French, Slovak and English descent. Similarly, he attended Lady Bird Johnson High School. However, he could not learn more in the school as he was homeschooled at his grandmother’s house.

Austin Mahone’s Career

In 2010, Mahone began his career as an avid YouTuber.Moreover, he then gained a fan base through some of the impressive covers of popular songs and amusing sketches.  The following year he posted a video covering Justin Bieber‘s Mistletoe.

In 2013, he released a song named What About Love. The music video won the MTV’s Artists to watch at MTV Video Music Awards. Further, he was selected as one of the openings acts of Taylor Swift‘s Red Tour.

Moreover, he was picked as Elvis Duran’s Artist of the Month. He was also featured on NBC’s Today show broadcast nationally in the United States. He also performed lady on Pitbull’s New Years Revolution. Since then he has performed at two Dolce & Gabbana fashion shows in Italy with his new work.
