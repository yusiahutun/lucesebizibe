100+ quotes about checking up on someone (for friends and loved ones)

August 2024 · 11 minute read

It's important to check up on our close friends and families occasionally and hear how they are faring. However, it may not always be possible to be the literal shoulder to lean on when loved ones are going through a tough time due to natural barriers such as distance. In this case, sending text quotes about checking up on someone will show you care for them.

Adult friendships are hard to maintain, especially whenever they're tested by barriers such as distance. It's important to make it a habit to always check up on those you love, not just when they're going through a rough time but also in times of laughter and happiness.
