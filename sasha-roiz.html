Sasha Roiz is a Canadian actor. Sasha Roiz is well-known for his role as Sam Adama in the science-fiction TV series Caprica and as Captain Sean Renard in the American fantasy TV series Grimm.

Early Life and Childhood

Sasha Roiz was born on October 21, 1973, in the city of Tel Aviv, Israel, to Russian Jewish parents. According to his birthdate, he is 48 years old under the zodiac sign of Libra. Regarding his nationality, he holds Canadian nationality and belongs to the Jewish ethnicity.

Talking about his family background, he is the son of Jacob Roiz (Father) and Rima Roiz (Mother). His father, Jacob was an engineer. As of now, he is the CEO of the company CEATI.

His mother, Rima is the director of the same company. Regarding his siblings, he has only one brother Elan Roiz who is the Executive vice-president of the company CEATI.

In 1980, his parents moved to Montreal, Canada when he was seven years old. There, he studied history before joining a theatre school in Montreal. Later, he graduated from Guilford School of Acting in Guildford, Surrey.

Career

Talking about his career, he has starred in several popular television dramas, including CSI: Miami, NCIS, House, Terminator: The Sarah Connor Chronicles, Lie To Me, and The Mentalist. He had a recurring role as Marcus Diamond on Warehouse 13.

Moreover, he has also appeared in numerous films. In 2005, he appeared as Jason Elias in the action movie Assault on Precinct 13. The following year, he was cast as Det. Kaller in 16 Blocks. In the same year, he starred as Donald Tilson in the thriller movie Man of the Year.

Similarly, he appeared as Master Sergeant Lubitchich in the movie Unthinkable in 2010.

In 2012, he appeared as Tom in the American science fiction thriller movie Extracted. Likewise, in 2014 he played a leading role as Proculus in the epic romantic historical disaster film Pompeii.
