Professional Life

Mary Kay Kleist is the meteorologist for 5 p.m., 6 p.m., and 10 p.m. newscasts on CBS 2 Chicago. She joined the station in 2002 and became a Certified Broadcast Meteorologist in 2007.

Being a Certified Broadcast Meteorologist is the highest distinction in this industry.

She had begun after her graduation. At first, she had begun working with WJCL-TV situated in Savannah, Georgio, as the weekend weather anchor and general assignment reporter. Similarly, she worked for two years until 1992 at WJCL-TV.

In the year 1995, she moved to Tampa, Florida to work for WFLA-TV as a meteorologist, where she worked from 1995-1999.

Likewise, she had even served as a weather reporter for The Tampa Tribune and as an anchor for WFLA-AM Radio. Then, since the year 1999, she has worked for WXYZ-TV in Detroit, Miami, as a meteorologist.

Moreover, she joined CBS 2 Chicago, where she has been working as a reputed meteorologist until now since the year 2002.

Awards and Achievements

Mary Kay Kleist won 3 Emmys in 2015. They were respectively for coverage of the April tornados, a severe weather special, and the best on-camera weather anchor.

Including these awards, she has also earned several local Emmy Awards at CBS 2 and also an AMS Seal of Approval. Alongside this, she has also bagged the NWA Broadcasting Seal of Approval.
