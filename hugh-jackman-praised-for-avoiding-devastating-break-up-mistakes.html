Hugh Jackman praised for avoiding devastating break-up mistakes

March 2024 · 3 minute read

It is not easy for celebrities to go through a break-up, especially after spending decades with a partner and building a life together in front of the public eye. So when Hugh Jackman’s divorce was announced, fans mourned another epic love story ending as the couple shared almost 27 years of marital bliss.

Hugh Jackman’s break-up sent shockwaves on the Internet. Months later, fans are praising the healthy ways the movie star is coping with the emotional loss and being an example of how to deal with a breakup maturely. Unlike many celebrity couples, the Wolverine star did not choose to go for a quick rebound, but rather focused on healing and going on with his daily life.

Hugh Jackman escapes dangerous break-up mistakes

Hollywood‘s most eligible bachelor Hugh Jackman surely knows how to avoid rookie break-up mistakes. The 55-year-old celebrity has officially called it quits with wife Deborra Lee in September, and their official statement claimed that the star couple is ending things ‘amicably.’

Ever since the divorce announcement, fans have spotted Hugh Jackman out and about in public engaging in daily activities and even accompanying friends to events.

The Hollywood heartthrob was seen with Ryan Reynolds, Blake Lively, and Taylor Swift attending a match of Kansas City Chiefs recently.

As per relationship experts, breakups are easier to deal with when one can find positive aspects of life and carry forward with bringing new elements while taking their due time to grieve the loss, and looks like, the Wolverine actor is on the right track!

Staying positive amid the worst

After building a life together with a significant partner, a breakup can feel like the worst that can happen, but relationship experts emphasize the importance of seeking positivity while going through such a tough time, and Hugh is doing exactly that.

The Grammy Award-winning artist was recently spotted volunteering at God’s Love charity kitchen in New York City. Hugh has been known for his charitable works all throughout his career, as he comes from a troubled childhood home and knows what pain looks like from early on.

Rumors say that the actor is also focusing on writing a memoir, where he will be opening up about many unknown chapters of his life for the first time.

Focusing on what makes you grounded

From taking a trip around New York City on his bicycle to exploring nature, Hugh’s Instagram story is a walking-talking reminder of what healing in progress can look like. The actor is also seeking the help of music therapy as he attended the Global Citizen Festival in Central Park.

The actor also celebrated his birthday recently and posted a snap from the special day with a delicious cocktail in hand on Instagram. Hugh being Hugh did not forget to express his gratitude to fans for the out pour of wishes.

Leaning onto friendship

Hugh seems surrounded by his close friends, be it his ‘best friend’ Ryan Reynolds or Blake Lively. The actor even took to Instagram to share an adorable, blurry selfie with Swift’s gang from the Kansas City Chiefs match.

With the positive vibes around the actor, fans are hopeful that the X-Men star will be able to heal from the difficult time and come out as a better person.
