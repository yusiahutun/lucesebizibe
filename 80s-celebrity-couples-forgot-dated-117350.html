Drew Barrymore and Corey Feldman Plus More 80s Celebrity Couples You Forgot All About

July 2024 · 3 minute read

It's hard to keep up with the many relationships that fester in Hollywood. Remember that time Drew Barrymore and Corey Feldman dated as cute teens in 1989? We forgot — and probably so have you.

Click through the gallery to see celebrity couples from the '80s that you have long forgot about.

1 of 13

Drew Barrymore and Corey Feldman

Corey wrote in his memoir Coreyography that the two dated as teens back in 1989.

2 of 13

Kim Basinger and Prince

The two dated briefly in 1989. "I don’t really have boundaries, so I enjoyed that time of my life," Kim said. "If there’s someone I connect with, we’ll go on these rides together. So that was a neat time in my life."

3 of 13

Tommy Lee and Heather Locklear

Before Tommy started his famous relationship with Pamela Anderson, he was married to Heather from 1986 to 1994.

4 of 13

Matthew Broderick and Helen Hunt

Before he met Sarah Jessica Parker, Matthew briefly dated Helen back in 1987.

5 of 13

Susan Sarandon and Sean Penn

Despite their 14 year age difference, the two had a brief fling back in 1984.

6 of 13

Diana Ross and Gene Simmons

If you think this couple is shocking, wait until you hear how they met! The two hooked up while Gene was still dating Cher.

"One Christmas I asked [Cher] what I should buy her," Gene said. "She said, 'Call my friend Diana Ross, she will tell you exactly what I like as she is my best friend'. So I called Diana up and we went shopping. Then our feelings for each other developed very fast and we started a relationship together."

7 of 13

Sylvester Stallone and Brigitte Nielsen

The two briefly married in 1985 after only knowing each other for a few months. They divorced 19 months later. “If I would go back in time, I shouldn’t have married him,” Brigitte said. “And he shouldn’t have married me!"

8 of 13

Marlee Matlin and John Stamos

The two dated briefly in the '80s and are still friends.

9 of 13

Scott Baio and Pamela Anderson

The two dated in the late '80s/early '90s and were even briefly engaged.

10 of 13

Brad Pitt and Robin Givens

The two had a torrid affair in 1989. Mike Tyson, Robin's husband at the time, even caught the two in bed together. "I was mad as hell," Mike said. "I was going to…you should have saw [Brad's] face when he saw me."

11 of 13

Molly Ringwald and Beastie Boy's Ad-Rock

The two dated briefly while working together on the movie The Pick-Up Artist.

12 of 13

Brooke Shields and Nicolas Cage

The two dated briefly in 1987 shortly before Nicolas' breakout role in Moonstruck.
