Super Collagen Mask

In a Vogue interview last year, Stewart’s facialist Carmela Barabas revealed that the Mario Badescu Super Collagen Mask is one of Stewart’s “favorite products” since it “restores skin texture and skin elasticity.” As the name suggests, it’s made with collagen to hydrate and smooth the skin, as well as red algae extract to lock in moisture and kaolin (aka white clay) to remove excess oil and impurities. For best results, the brand recommends applying a thin layer of the mask to clean skin, allowing it to sit for 20 minutes, then rinsing it off with lukewarm water.
