He plays action man Peter Bishop in hit show Fringe.

But Joshua Jackson looked far from fit as he hobbled through an airport in Los Angeles on crutches on Monday.

The 35-year-old was grimly determined to make his flight to London with glamorous German squeeze Diane Kruger, though it looked like he had taken the liberty of arriving for his flight in pyjamas.

Not his best foot forward: Joshua Jackson hobbled through an airport in Los Angeles with crutches on Monday after seemingly injuring his toe

The Dawson's Creek star looked crook as he made his way through the notoriously busy transport hub, and was no doubt finding the journey more challenging than usual.

And it seems he may have been the victim of a bizarre gardening accident, as he was wearing a special brace around his right foot with the only visible bandaging applied to his big toe.

No doubt his dutiful lady Diane, 37, clad in a Rag & Bone jacket and sweatpants, will have been going out of her way to help her stricken beau as they prepared to make their transatlantic flight.

Leading the way: Joshua still managed to charge ahead despite the fact his girlfriend Diane Kruger, pictured in a straw hat with Rag & Bone denim jacket and sweatpants, was uninjured

Toerag: Diane seemed more concerned with checking her phone than making sure Joshua was alright

Sign of the times: It seems the celebrities were getting special treatment by flouting the No Entry warning

However, she recently made him a figure of fun after revealing her mother used her first meeting with Joshua as an opportunity to quiz him on his 2002 assault arrest.

The German actress said her old dear Maria-Theresa thought it 'would be cute' to pull out his police picture before interrogating him about the incident, where he was accused of scuffling with a security guard at a Carolina Hurricanes ice hockey match.

She told Jimmy Kimmel: 'I thought we were doing so good and my mom says "ah, wait I have something for you."

Downcast: Joshua did not look in a good mood despite giving fans a cheap thrill with his airport appearance

Any artificial plates or limbs? The security guard would have surely been tempted to crack a gag after seeing his Spinal Tap-esque injury

'She goes into the office and comes back with this folder of internet clippings of Josh with his mugshot.

'She thought it would be cute to go, "so I see you've been arrested in 1997 at a hockey game. Who's fault was it?"

'You can imagine. We were there for three days, we did not speak for two. It was not good.'
