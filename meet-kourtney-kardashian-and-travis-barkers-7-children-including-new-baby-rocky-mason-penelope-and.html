Meet Kourtney Kardashian and Travis Barkers 7 children, including new baby Rocky: from Mason, Pen

May 2024 · 3 minute read

Thankfully, things worked out and now they have plenty to celebrate with their newborn child. According to E!, the Blink-182 drummer had already settled on the baby’s name before it was born – Rocky Thirteen Barker.

Parenting is nothing new for these lovebirds though, as they currently share six kids between them from previous marriages.

So who are all of Kourtney Kardashian, 44, and Travis Barker’s, 47, children, from oldest to youngest?

1. Atiana De La Hoya, 24

Atiana De La Hoya, 24, is the daughter of Barker’s ex-wife, 1995’s Miss USA runner-up Shanna Moakler, and boxing legend Oscar De La Hoya. She was five years old when Barker tied the knot with her mum, reports People. From 2005 to 2006, she joined her family to star in Meet the Barkers.

According to Hola, she joined the modelling biz at 14 and was in a reality show in 2017, called Growing Up Supermodel. She’s also a talented artist, and once custom painted a bag for Machine Gun Kelly.

2. Landon Barker, 20

Barker and Moakler’s first son together, Landon, was born in October 2003. He appears to be following in his dad’s musical footsteps, having released songs like “Trust” and “Holiday” over the years. “A lot of people either do exactly what their parents did or the complete opposite, and I’m one of those who wants to do exactly what my dad did,” he told People in October.

The 20-year-old, who is currently dating TikTok influencer Charli D’Amelio, has also dabbled in acting and guest-starred on Machine Gun Kelly’s musical drama Downfalls High alongside Sydney Sweeney and Blackbear.

3. Alabama Barker, 17

The youngest child of Barker and Moakler was first introduced to the world when she appeared on season two of Meet the Barkers in December 2005, per People

The teen also appears to be taking the musical route, while her social media platforms have racked up millions of followers. Alabama is also pals with Jodie Woods, the younger sister of Kylie Jenner’s former best friend Jordyn Woods, according to the same source.

4. Mason Disick, 13

Kourtney Kardashian had three kids during her long-time relationship with ex-boyfriend Scott Disick.

Their first child together was Mason, who was born in 2009. Rarely seen in public, the 13-year-old seems to share a close bond with both of his parents. His middle name “Dash” is inspired by his late grandfather Robert Kardashian’s nickname, People reported.

5. Penelope Disick, 11

Kardashian and Disick’s only daughter together, Penelope, was born in 2012. Also referred to as “P”, the youngster regularly appears on her mum’s Instagram and hangs out with her aunt Kim Kardashian’s daughter North West.

6. Reign Disick, 8

Reign, who’s eight, is also relatively low-key compared to the rest of the Kardashian clan. Born in 2014, Kardashian and Disick keep him out of the limelight for now.

7. Rocky Thirteen Barker, born November 2023

In November this year, Kourtney Kardashian and her husband Travis Barker brought a new baby boy into the world. Kardashian announced the pregnancy back in June at a Blink-182 concert, where she held up a sign saying “Travis I’m Pregnant”.

It’s believed that their new son’s name is Rocky after a comment Barker made on a podcast before his birth, though it’s not been officially confirmed since his arrival.
