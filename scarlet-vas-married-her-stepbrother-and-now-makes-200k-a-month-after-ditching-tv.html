Scarlet Vas married her 'stepbrother' and now makes $200k a month after ditching TV

April 2024 · 2 minute read

Former Neighbors actress Scarlet Vas married her “stepbrother” Tayo Ricci and now makes $200k a month.

If you’re a fan of Neighbors then you probably know Australian actress Scarlet Vas better as Mishti Sharma, her police officer character in the beloved TV soap. The 28-year-old made a splash on the show back in 2017 but would announce her departure from it just one year later. You may be wondering what the celebrity been up to since leaving the popular series behind, and the answer gets pretty steamy…

Scarlet Vas married her ‘stepbrother’ Tayo Ricci

Scarlet married her “stepbrother” Tayo in 2023, sharing a glimpse into the luxurious ceremony on her Instagram, where she has 2.2 million followers.

Tayo, like his wife, is a YouTuber and influencer. On the other hand, he’s also associated with the boxing world.

Something they also share in common is working on the adult content site OnlyFans, both boasting their own respective following on the platform.

“He’s my stepbrother and Australian and we’ve known each other for a very long time,” Scarlet said previously during an appearance on the Uncensored podcast with Mario Adrion.

Tayo was also present for the interview and was asked whether they help each other with making adult content. “I would say partially, yeah,” he revealed. “Definitely on hers.”

“Yeah, he always makes appearances on mine,” Scarlet clarified, although Taro explained: “To be honest, to the standard that people expect, honestly no. Like close family friends would assume we’re doing [adult vidoes] because we’re making that much money but we’re not.”

All the same, Scarlet assured listeners: “There’s some cheeky stuff.

Tayo added: “I’m not gonna lie, when I was young, I fantasized about it. It felt so wrong till it felt so right.”

Scarlet makes $200k a month

Making the leap from TV to the subscription site, Scarlet revealed during the Uncensored interview from 2023 she’s making plenty of cash. At first, she was having a tough time, though.

“I’m in the 0.01%, like the lowest you can go on OF,” she acknowledged. “But every month’s been getting better. Like last month was my best month. I hit almost $200k and then this month I’m going to do well over $200k.

However, the transition didn’t start so lucrative: “… To start, I wasn’t making a lot.”

She charges $24.99 a month on the site and has a huge following as one of its biggest stars. Her husband, Tayo, offers monthly subscriptions for $19.99 at regular price.
