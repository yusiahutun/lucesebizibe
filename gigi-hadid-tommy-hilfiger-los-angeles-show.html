7 Things To Know About Gigi Hadid and Tommy Hilfiger's LA Fashion Show

June 2024 · 3 minute read

Last night, Gigi Hadid and Tommy Hilfiger joined forces for their second runway collaboration. The see-now, buy-now Spring 2017 show headed to the West Coast, marking the first time the fashion brand has ever shown in Los Angeles instead of New York. From a star-studded front row to an all-star model lineup, here's what you missed at the Tommy x Gigi show.

1) The show was staged at "Tommyland" on Venice Beach, marking Hilfiger's first time ever showing on the West Coast. Like the duo's larger-than-life spectacle at New York Fashion Week last season, this show set had a carnvial-meets-festival vibe with food vendors, games, pop-up shops selling Tommy x Gigi merch and more.

Getty Images

Getty Images

2) Unsurprisingly, Gigi opened and closed the show. For the first look, the supermodel wore a red, white and blue crop top, leather pants and a windbreaker. For the finale, she walked the runway in an ethereal maxi dress.

Getty Images

3) Lady Gaga made a splash in the the front row. Hot off the heels of her Super Bowl Halftime Show, the singer showed up wearing bedazzled face jewelry and no pants—giving some major old school Gaga vibes. Kris Jenner, Sofia Richie, Sereyah, Kaia Gerber and more also sat front row during the show.

Getty Images

4) Sister Bella Hadid and pals Hailey Baldwin, Joan Smalls and Stella Maxwell led the finale walk together. The model lineup, who all arrived to Los Angeles together aboard #AirTommy, also included Sara Sampaio, Devon Windsor, Alanna Arrington, Romee Strijd and more.

Getty Images

5) Gigi's younger brother, Anwar Hadid, ran out on the runway to give his sister a bouquet of flowers and a kiss at the end of the show—making for the cutest sibling moment.

After the show, Bella also posted a sweet message to her sister on Instagram, writing how proud she was of her:

6) Fergie performed at the after-party. The #TommyNow stage looked like a mini Coachella set for guests to get into the festival mood with music, food-stands, booths selling merch and more.

Getty Images

7) The Tommy x Gigi Spring 2017 collection is now available to shop and rent exclusively at armarium.com, marking the first time a see-now, buy-now collection has been available to rent.
