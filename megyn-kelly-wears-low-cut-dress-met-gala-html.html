Met Gala 2017: Megyn Kelly wears a low-cut dress

September 2024 Â· 4 minute read

The Met Gala is one of the fashion industry's most prestigious events and plays host to all manner of style sensations each year, so it's no wonder that Megyn Kelly decided to do something a bit different with her appearance before hitting the red carpet at the ball. 

Megyn, 46, made a move away from her trademark cropped hairstyle in favor of a long sleek ponytail, adding some heavy eye make-up to the look to complete the glamorous ensemble. 

As for fashion, the mother-of-three, who is due to make her debut at NBC later this month, wore a floor-length red floral dress which was nipped in at the waist with a black sash and featured a plunging neckline.

The low-cut dress also featured some pearl embellishments along the deep V, as well as some pretty embroidery across the entire design. 

She accessorized the look with a small box clutch and a pair of dazzling earrings.

Meanwhile her husband Douglas Brunt looked dapper in a classic black tuxedo, opting to model a very traditional look. 

Megyn's appearance on the red carpet comes just weeks before she is due to begin her new role at NBC, having already begun filming her first few big-hitting interviews - including several members of the Kardashian Jenner clan, whom Megyn is believed to have already sat down with for one of her first big NBC segments.

The former Fox News host and new network star was spotted in Los Angeles on Thursday heading in and out of the same studio where the Kardashian and Jenner women were taping a 10th anniversary special for Today. 

She was snapped as she and her two person squad made their way through the studio lot, and her masterful makeup and perfectly tousled blonde waves along with her ruffled crimson baby doll dress seemed like just the look for a day of keeping and catching up with the fashion-forward ladies.

Both the E! Reality show and NBC morning show are NBC universal properties.

The lambskin leather minidress she wore is Louis Vuitton and retails for $4,850, which with New York sales tax comes in at a whopping $5,280. 

Kelly posted a picture of herself outside 30 Rock on Twitter with the caption: 'First day on the job @NBCNews! On the air in June, but had so much fun meeting new colleagues today. Grateful. Happy'

Getting ready... The TV star was spotted in Los Angeles last week, where she is thought to have been sitting down with the Kardashians for an interview

There has been no official word from NBC on whether or not Megyn will be doing the interview, but TMZ confirmed she filmed a massive sit down with the five daughters and Kris at once- a rare group interview for the family. 

The former Fox News anchor had been laying low ever since she announced back in January that she would be spreading her feathers and heading over to NBC when her contract was up with the cable news leader, where she has worked for more than a decade.

However in recent weeks she has begun making more appearances at star-studded events, hitting the red carpet at The Hollywood Reporter Media event in New York last month, before heading to the Time 100 Most Influential People party days later.

And now the Met Gala, one of the most glittering events on New York's social calendar.
