Erin Dolan, in full, Erin Kate Dolan, is an American personality and ESPN Betting Analyst by profession. She has worked on radio shows and as an on-air radio personality. She is well-versed in sports betting knowledge and has recently been in the news because of the new or ongoing news or announcements in the betting world by ESPN.

Erin Dolan Bio, Age, Height, Husband, Net Worth, Wiki & More

Erin Dolan was born in June 1996 in Philadelphia Suburbs and she is a scholar student at the University of Oregon. She studied Bachelor of Science in Broadcast Journalism from 2014 to 2015. During her college years, she was a member of sports reporter for DuckTV and an Association for Women in Sports Media (AWSM) member.

Erin has also studied at Penn State University from 2016 to 2018. She studied for a Bachelor of Arts in Broadcast Journalism (sports concentration). At Penn State University, she was rewarded with various honors and rewards for the different categories like –

  • Received 2 Mid-Atlantic Emmy Nominations 
  • Received SPJ Award of Excellence in “Television Sports Reporting” 2017
  • She also received the honor of “Davis Ethics Award” from Penn State College of Communications 2017 
  • Received AWSM Scholarship winner with an internship at ESPN 2017 
  • Received “Harry Kalas Award” from the Philadelphia Broadcasters Foundation 2016 

Career

Erin began working at ESPN in December 2021 as a sports betting analyst. She is a regular news reporter for ESPN’s sports betting news and information program, Daily Wager. She also reports on daily programs and segments across the ESPN television, radio, and digital platforms. Before joining ESPN, Erin worked for a radio network at FanDuel and PointsBet sportsbooks, where she was responsible for creating sports betting content across their social channels.

She has also co-hosted various other shows like “Girls Who Bet,” “The Group Chat,” “The Early Line”, and others– that used to be televised on YouTube, Twitch, and SiriusXM, weekly or on weekdays, respectively. Erin began her professional life as a sports reporter for PHL17 in Philadelphia. She also worked as a sideline reporter for the Philadelphia Wings.

Erin is a sports betting analyst working for Daily Wager, SportsCenter, This Just In, and various linear programming. She is a regular contender for radio and digital shows and has hosted many other shows on television, like Snapchat. She has also contributed her time and energy to writing the various content on ESPN’s Chalk.
