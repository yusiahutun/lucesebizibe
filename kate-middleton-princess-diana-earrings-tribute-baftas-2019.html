Kate Middleton Wore a Pair of Princess Diana's Earrings on the BAFTA Red Carpet

May 2024 Â· 2 minute read

Stepping out for the BAFTAs this weekend alongside Prince William, Kate Middleton looked stunning in a one-shoulder white gown and a sparkly pair of Jimmy Choo pumps.

Related Story

And completing the look, the Duchess of Cambridge wore an extra special pair of earrings which belonged to her late mother-in-law, Princess Diana. Sparkling as she walked on the red carpet, the dangling pearl earrings were the perfect choice to wear alongside Kate's angelic white gown.

As pointed out by Twitter user @TeaCambridges, this is the first time that Kate has worn this particular pair of earrings. Twitter user @hrhkatemeghan concurred that Kate is wearing Princess Diana's Diamond and South Sea Pearl dangle earrings, which the late royal was photographed wearing on several occasions. Here she is seen wearing them in 1995:

Tim Graham//Getty Images

Princess Diana at Bridgewater House in London on November 20, 1995.

Pascal Le Segretain//Getty Images

Kate has often worn pieces from Diana's extensive jewelry collection, as has Meghan Markle since she joined the royal family in May 2018. Just last month, the Duchess of Cambridge paid tribute to her late mother-in-law during a visit to Dundee, by wearing Diana's Sapphire earrings, which perfectly matched her engagement ring.

Kate is also a fan of channeling Princess Diana's iconic style. From her smart suit moments, to taking inspiration from her polka dot dresses, Kate is definitely inspired by her late mother-in-law's incredible wardrobe.

As always, it's heartwarming to know that Princess Diana's style is being kept alive by her daughter-in-law.
