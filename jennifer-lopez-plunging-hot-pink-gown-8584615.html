See Jennifer Lopez's Plunging Hot Pink Gown

May 2024 Â· 2 minute read

As the week goes on, Jennifer Lopez's press tour looks just keep getting better. From a plunging royal blue jumpsuit with a cape-style coat to a gold bra covered in roses, J.Lo, who is currently promoting her new album This Is Me...Now and its accompanying musical film, has been serving look after look these past few days and her momentum is showing no signs of slowing down.  

Case in point? On Friday, Lopez closed out the week in a hot pink gown with a high slit that competed against its ultra-deep neckline while visiting The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon. The halter-style dress plunged all the way down her midriff before tying in a knot at the navel and revealing a high leg slit in the center underneath. J.Lo filled in her bare swathe of skin on her bodice with a long diamond necklace and finished off her accessories with a pair of silver peep-toe platform heels and a sparkly pink-and-blue cocktail ring.  

As for glam, the singer-slash-actress wore her light brown hair in waist-length mermaid waves with a middle part and added a swipe of pink lipgloss and fuchsia-colored blush to her cheeks.

During her appearance, J.Lo spoke about her new album and upcoming tour, teasing that it could be her "last hurrah." "This project took so much out of me," she told Fallon. "You're just so obsessed with something and you can't stop and you're just, like every detail and every thing. And it turned into the Amazon Original musical that I made. And then it turned into this documentary. And it was like all of these things together. And every single lyric I was part of on this record and I wrote, and it was just draining."

She added, "And you think to yourself, 'This is it. This is my last hurrah.'"
