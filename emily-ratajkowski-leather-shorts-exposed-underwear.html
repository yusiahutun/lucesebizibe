Emily Ratajkowski's Party Look Includes Tiny Leather Hot Shorts

March 2024 · 2 minute read

The chilly New York winter did not deter Emily Ratajkowski from wearing the perfect sexy biker-chic party look to celebrate her collaboration with Miu Miu this Tuesday.

The model arrived at the brand’s boutique to mark the launch of her Miu Miu Select, a highly personalized collection of pieces she curated from the latest Miu Miu collection. The selection will be available in the New York stores, as well as in Miami and at California’s South Coast Plaza.

EmRata’s Miu Miu ensemble for the event included a shiny black leather set made up of a boxy short collared jacket with cropped sleeves, faux pockets, and gold buttons, plus matching hot shorts. The teeny-tiny leather shorts were cinched at the waist with a built-in belt with a gold buckle featuring the brand's logo. Under the shorts, Ratajkowski wore white Miu Miu underwear, which she left exposed at the top, expertly repping the ongoing lingerie-on-the-outside trend.

MEGA//Getty Images

Ratajkowski paired the leather set with glossy, pointy black kitten-heel slingbacks with thin straps all over, plus a mini leather bag. For her jewelry, the My Body author matched the gold accents of her outfit with some gold hoop earrings. She wore natural makeup, including a glossy nude lip, and had her hair down, straight.

“@miumiu girl ♾️ #miumiuselect,” EmRata captioned an Instagram post featuring photos of herself in the look.

Miu Miu also shared candid photos from the event—including of the model signing autographs—on its official Instagram page.
