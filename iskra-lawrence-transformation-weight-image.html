Iskra Lawrence shares transformation weight image to promote body positivity

February 2024 · 2 minute read

Iskra Lawrence has shared an empowering image of her weight transformation to promote self-care and body confidence.

The two pictures were taken a decade apart, showing the blogger in the early stages of her modelling career, then 10 years later when she was feeling happier and "grateful for this body I call my home".

"I used to seek approval from the fashion industry and tried to be 'perfect,'" she wrote in a lengthy Instagram caption. "I thought if I looked like 'her' (an unrealistic beauty ideal), I’d be happy, successful and loved.

"All I found was failure (because you can’t change who you are), emptiness (because my time and energy was being used up trying to achieve something completely self absorbed and shallow sacrificing doing things I loved) and unhappiness (because no restrictive diet or abusive exercise feels good)."

Lawrence was dropped as by her model agency, aged 15, after being deemed 'too fat', and has since made her mission to change the way women feel about their bodies. She has recently launched a health, fitness and self-care programme called EveryBodyWithIskra, which offers support instead of judgement and unrealistic goals.

"I don’t have all the answers and I’m not ever trying to change anyone or tell people to do this and that etc," she wrote. "But I felt the need to create this as I wish I could have had this when I was struggling and if I’m able to use that to help even one person even better."
