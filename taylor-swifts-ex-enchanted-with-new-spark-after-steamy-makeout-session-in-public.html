Taylor Swift's 'ex' enchanted with new spark after steamy makeout session in public

July 2024 · 2 minute read

Taylor Swift’s rumored ex-fling Matty Healy seems enchanted by someone new as the 1975 frontman is spotted locking lips in New York City.

Matty Healy is known to have a rather long list of girlfriends and rumored flings which include FKA Twigs, Gabriella Brooks, Halsey, Meredith Mickelson, and more. Matty was linked with Taylor Swift this year in May. It came after the About You singer attended the Eras Tour in Nashville and was even apparently seen touching his heart when Taylor played Lover!

Taylor Swift’s ‘ex’ fling Matty Healy seen making out in public

Months after Taylor Swift’s close source told the press the whirlwind romance of Healy and Swift was ‘only casual’ the Hollywood romance had ended. The singer has now been seen taking a stroll in New York City with model Gabbriette Bechtel.

The pair did not shy away from making their romance public as they were seen locking lips and embracing each other in tight hugs.

Internet reacts to Matty Healy’s new romance, “go live my dream”

From Swifties (Taylor Swift fans) to Matty Healy stans, music listeners have mixed reactions to his new romance.

An enthusiastic fan quipped on X (formerly Twitter), “Yes girl (Matty Healy) go live my dream (date Gabriette)!”

While some fans are happy to see Matty Healy’s new girl, others are heartbroken.

One posted: “Today is my last day on earth” and another chose Swift’s lyrics to express their feelings, gushing, “taking my time, taking my time, CAUSE YOU TOOK EVERYTHING FROM ME!”

“Gabbriette and Taylor Swift in the same incarnation? God is not fair,” a fourth fan posted.

Who is Gabbriette Bechtel?

Gabbriette Bechtel is a rising model who is popular for her edgy style, grungy fashion, and really sharp eyebrows.

The Instagram model and influencer has worked with a number of luxury brands including Balenciaga, Isabel Marant, Diesel, and more.

Along with fashion, Gabbriette loves posting food content and is passionate about music. She was also part of the band Nasty Cherry before the group seemingly split in 2021.
