20 dumbest animals in the world and why they aren't smart

May 2024 · 8 minute read

Some animals have portrayed ludicrous behaviours, from burying their heads in the sand to hide from enemies to deserting their young ones. Such behaviours have led to people listing them as the dumbest animals in the world.

Which are the dumbest animals in the world that you know? Why are they considered foolish? Here is what you need to know about the dumbest animals globally.

Dumbest animals in the world

There are numerous animals in the animal kingdom. Some are smart, some are lazy, and some are downright dumb. Below are some of the animals considered dumb.

1. Sloths

Sloths are sluggish tree-dwellers of Central and South America. They spend their lives in tropical rainforests, moving through the canopy and munching on leaves, twigs, and buds.

They are considered stupid animals. because they spend most of their time sleeping on tree branches. However, they never poop on the trees. They descend once a week to poop on the ground and then go back up.

2. Wild turkeys

The wild turkey is an upland ground bird native to North America. These birds can’t help but stare uncontrollably at the sky, even when it’s raining. This is the dumbest thing on earth because it sometimes causes their death.

3. Killdeer

The Killdeer is a large plover found in the America. The bird is stupid because it rushes around, screaming its call, attracting the attention of its predators. Interestingly, when its enemy approaches, it stands still and bobs up and down. It also builds its nest on the ground, making itself easy prey.

4. Secretary Bird

The secretary bird is a large and mostly terrestrial bird of prey. It is usually found in the open grasslands and savanna of the sub-Saharan region. This bird is considered foolish refuses to use its wings despite being able to fly just fine.

5. Ostrich

Ostriches are large flightless birds. They are the heaviest living birds and lay the largest eggs of any live land animal. They have oversized eyes and undersized brains.

It is a common believe that the size of brain should be in proportion to the size of the animal to maintain and increase intelligence. Therefore, considering the size of the brain of an ostrich, it would be right to assume that it is not intelligent.

6. Komodo Dragon

The Komodo dragon is a member of the monitor lizard family Varanidae. It is endemic to the Indonesian islands of Komodo, Rinca, Flores, and Gili Motang. It is the largest species of lizard. It grows to a maximum length of 3 m and can weigh up to 70 kg.

7. Flamingos

Flamingos are famous for their bright pink feathers, stilt-like legs, and S-shaped neck. Their favourite foods include shrimp, snails, and plantlike water organisms called algae.

Flamingos are considered foolish because, despite having a pair of legs, they use only one of them to stand and rest most of the time. Sometimes they also try to catch fish with their beaks flipped upside down in the water.

8. Koala

The koala is a stocky animal with a large head and vestigial tail. These animals are found in Australia. It has the smallest brain-to-body ratio among animals. The animal's brain is also almost entirely smooth, meaning their cognitive abilities are few and far between.

9. Goblin shark

The goblin shark is a rare species of deep-sea shark. It is sometimes called a living fossil. It is often found off the coast of Japan.

Goblin sharks have shovel-like snouts, flabby bodies, and tails with weakly developed lower lobes. They are considered stupid because they are lazy and sluggish that they don’t want to hunt. Instead, they wait for the prey to find them, which may lead to their death.

10. Cane Toads

The cane toad is a giant terrestrial true toad. It is native to South and mainland Central America. Its diet includes both living and dead matter. They are considered incredibly dumb because of their diet. They will eat literally anything.

11. Horned Lizard

The horned lizard is a member of Phrynosoma, which is a genus of North American lizards. They have distinct characters which differentiate them from other lizards. They mainly eat spiders, sowbugs, and ants.

12. Slow Loris

The slow loris is categorised in the group of several species of nocturnal strepsirrhine primates. They may be cute, bijoux, and big-eyed, but they are also toxic, fractious, and potentially lethal.

When slow loris defends itself from predators, its pace rarely disturbs foliage. However, within a matter of seconds, it stops moving. When its predator approaches, it freezes and hides its face. This is the reason why it is seen as a foolish animal.

13. Jerboa

Jerboa is found in Asia and North Africa. It looks like a rat but has tiny legs that allow it to hop, just like a kangaroo. It skips, jumps, and runs using bipedal locomotion.

Its movement is characterised by unpredictable changes in direction and speed. The dumb nature of Jerbao becomes glaring because of their inability to master effectively how to make use of their hind legs. Allegedly, it takes about 11 weeks to master how to use them.

14. Giraffe

The giraffe is not only tall but also the dumbest animal on earth. They have long necks to help them eat the leaves of tall trees. Its natural habitat is East Africa, but it is also found in other parts of the continent.

They are considered stupid creatures because they were deemed incapable of making sounds to communicate.

15. Lilac-b*reasted roller

The lilac-b*reasted roller is an African bird of the roller family. It is widely distributed in Southern and Eastern Africa. It prefers open woodland and savanna, and it is absent in treeless places.

It is among the dumbest animals on the planet. Its inclination towards tall perches, even in places where nesting sites are almost non-existent, will leave you wondering.

Additionally, this bird does not attempt to fly away when someone comes close to it. As a result, it is an easy target for predators.

16. Norway Lemming

Norway lemming is a common species of lemming found in northern Fennoscandia. The rodent dwells in tundra and fells and prefers to live near water.

The animal is considered stupid because it will attack other wild animals far bigger than it. In some cases, this leads to its demise.

17. Kakapo

The Kakapo is also known as the owl parrot. It is a species of large, flightless, nocturnal, ground-dwelling parrot. It is mostly found in New Zealand. It is one of the dumbest animals out there.

The Kakapo freezes in fear as soon as it spots its predator. It does not try to run to safety. It is also a poor breeder. These are some of the reasons why it is considered foolish.

18. Fulmar

Fulmars are members of a group of birds known as tubenoses or petrels. It is considered one of the animals that are dumb in the world. This is so because they are bad parents.
