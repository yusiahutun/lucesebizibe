20 short prayers for your family during the difficult times

June 2024 · 11 minute read

Prayers are part of the Christian life. Many people turn to prayer when they are going through troubled times, when they want to thank God for whatever they have achieved, or as a routine. This helps with their spiritual life and journey. Having a prayerful family is a blessing for those that believe. Short prayers for your family during difficult times shows that you care for your loved ones.

A prayer a day keeps the devil away. During difficult times, all may seem not to work, and the devil may seem at work. However, such times require you to be close to God through prayers. Whenever you pray, the Lord listens.

Short prayers for your family

Sometimes prayers for your family during difficult times don't have to be long. You may not have many words, but even the shortest devotion reach the ears of God. So never hesitate to speak to Him at any time.

To cover each family member from physical or emotional torture, a prayer for their protection and guidance is a sure way. You can use these family prayers for strength during difficult times as your guide.

As each of us goes about the daily routine in our lives, we ask that you guide our every step. We don't know what each of our lives holds in-store, but you do. Go before us and make straight our paths. Please give us the wisdom to make the right choices. Please give us the strength to keep going when life becomes difficult. We will rest in who you are and that nothing is too complicated for you. We thank you for all you have done and will do for us. Our lives are set apart for You. Amen.
