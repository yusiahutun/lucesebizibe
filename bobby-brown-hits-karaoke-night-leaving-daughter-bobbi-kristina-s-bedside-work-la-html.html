Bobby Brown performs on stage after leaving daughter Bobbi Kristina's bedside

June 2024 · 5 minute read

Bobby Brown just recently left his daughter, Bobbi Kristina's side for the first time in months.

On Friday he jetted from Atlanta ahead of Saturday's scheduled appearance at the Soul Food Festival, after which he was spotted taking the stage to perform.

Putting an end to the vigil, 46-year-old Bobby's pregnant wife Alicia Etheredge was also back to work at the weekend when she was seen selling the musician's BBQ sauce on a food stall at the food festival.

Alicia was confirmed as pregnant with their second child - after five-year-old Cassius - last month, one week after Bobbi Kristina was found unconscious in a bathtub and sent to Emory University Hospital in Georgia, where the couple have been by her side ever since. 

TMZ reported that Bobby took his mind off the events for the first time this weekend when he was joined at the event by seven or eight male friends.

He was spotted taking the stage at Paoli's Pizzeria and Piano Bar in Woodland Hills, California from 9pm until around midnight.

Prior to the appearance TMZ had broken the news that Bobby would be leaving Bobbi Kristina's side because he had previously committed to the upcoming events. 

Bobbi, the daughter of Whitney Houston and also one of Bobby Brown's five children, remains hospitalized and in a coma after being found on January 31.

According to TMZ, the singer is due to play three shows Down Under with Treach and Kay-Gee of Naughty By Nature at the end of May, appearances that will replace gigs he cancelled in March to be with Bobbi. 

Even though he doesn't want to go, the promoters are holding the distraught father to his contractual obligation, it has been claimed.

'Nick is responding well to rehab treatment with no relapses...The quiet time is good for him. He needed it,' the source said.
