Jamie Carragher makes 'slow motion' dig at Liverpool star after Man Utd defeat

February 2024 · 3 minute read

Jamie Carragher has taken aim at Liverpool forward Cody Gakpo after Sunday's 4-3 FA Cup quarter-final defeat to Manchester United.

The Reds suffered defeat by conceding a goal in the final minute of extra-time thanks to Amad Diallo’s superb finish. Jurgen Klopp’s side missed out on a spot in the semis despite taking the lead twice throughout the affair, with Alexis Mac Allister and Mohamed Salah scoring a quickfire double on the stroke of half-time after Scott McTominay had opened the scoring.

Antony’s goal took the game to extra-time, where Harvey Elliott thought he had sealed a huge win for the visiting Reds. But Liverpool squandered another lead as Marcus Rashford and Diallo sealed their fate late on at Old Trafford.

Despite conceding four goals, Liverpool legend and pundit Carragher did not call out the Reds defenders. He took to social media to instead slam Gakpo, who was brought on for Salah in the 77th minute.

“Gakpo plays like the game is in slow motion,” the former centre-back brutally tweeted. Yet others were in agreement.

One Liverpool fan replied: “He plays like his boots are too heavy for him,” while another tweeted: “Bro has concrete in his boots.”

A third added: “Killed every attack we have had. He is awful,” while a fourth fumed: “What a useless performance from him.”

Others vented: “He is just very frustrating to watch,” and: “He ain’t a Liverpool kind of player.”

Gakpo lost six out of his 10 duels during his 43 minute cameo. He took 24 touches but just one of those came in the opposition box, while he only made one successful dribble and was dispossessed twice.

The 24-year-old forward has scored 13 goals in 42 games across all competitions this season. Just five of those have come in the league, with the Netherlands international netting four in the Europa League and four in the EFL Cup.
