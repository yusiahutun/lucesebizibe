KMTC courses and qualifications, fees, admission requirements

April 2024 · 5 minute read

Kenya Medical Training College (KMTC) is a Kenyan college under the Ministry of Health. It offers multiple health-related courses across multiple branches across the country. Every year, the institution admits thousands of students for certificate, diploma, post-diploma, and short courses. Discover all KMTC courses and qualifications today.

TABLE OF CONTENTS

KMTC trains various professionals in the health and medical sector at certificate, diploma, and post-basic diploma levels. If you wish to study at this institution, you must familiarise yourself with the KMTC courses and qualifications.

KMTC qualifications

Only people who meet certain criteria should apply to join KMTC. The application requirements or entry criteria depend on the programme you wish to pursue or the level of training. Check the table below for more details.

LevelEntry requirementsCourse duration
CertificateA minimum aggregate of C- with a relevant combination of subjects (except Certificate in Health Records and InformationTechnology (in-service category only) and Nutrition and Dietetics that accept D+)2 years except for nursing which takes 2.5 years
DiplomaKCSE mean grade of C (or equivalent) with a relevant combination of subjects, e.g., English, Kiswahili, Biology, Biological Sciences, Mathematics, Chemistry, Physics and Physical Sciences3 years except for community health nursing which takes 3.5 years
Higher DiplomaHolders of basic diploma in relevant fields and at least two years working experience1 year
UpgradingHolders of a certificate in relevant fields and at least two years working experience2 years except for community health nursing which lasts 1.5 years
Short coursesIndividuals and groups interested in any course or courses can make a written request for arrangements to be made2 weeks – 3 months

Points to note for prospective students:

  • You are encouraged to check the relevant subject combinations for the course you are interested in.
  • You must have supporting documents to back up your application.
  • You must have valid identification documents and relevant academic and professional certificates when applying and reporting for admission.

When to apply for KMTC intake in 2024

KMTC holds two intakes every year. The first happens in March and the other in September. The KMTC application portal is not officially open for the March 2024 intake.

However, it remains accessible to prospective students. The March 2024 intake advert will be released soon, so you are encouraged to keep visiting the website. The application portal will be opened again later in the year for the September 2024 intake.

KMTC fee structure for certificate courses & others

The semester and annual fees for students are set by the ministry in charge. The ministry reserves the right to review the fees.

What are the grades required for KMTC?

Diploma students must have a KCSE mean grade of C, while certificate students must have a minimum KCSE aggregate of C- with a relevant combination of subjects for the course.

What are the requirements for a Diploma in Nursing at KMTC in 2024?

You must have a KCSE mean grade of C (or equivalent) with a relevant combination of subjects to pursue a Diploma in Nursing.

Which course can you do with C minus in KMTC?

You can pursue a certificate-level course with a KCSE aggregate of C-. Kindly note you must have the relevant combination of subjects to pursue various courses.
