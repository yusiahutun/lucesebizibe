Born and raised in California, Jamie Apody is an American journalist, reporter as well as a sports anchor who is currently serving as the sports anchor and reporter for WPVI TV and 6ABC since January 2006.

Jamie Apody’s Bio, Wiki

Jamie Apody was born on 7 April 1978 in California, Unites States of America. However, her hometown is Calabasas on the west coast. Since her childhood, she was a basketball lover and also practiced karate for 20 years. At the age of 13, Jamie even got her black belt in Tang Soo Do.

Regarding her educational qualification, she graduated from the University of Los Angeles with a Bachelor’s in Communication Studies in 2000. She is currently working as a sports anchor and reporter for WPVI TV and 6ABC since January 2006. Back in 1997, she worked as a sports producer of KNBC TV.

However, she shifted her career to the journalism sector as the reporter for Action News sports team in 2006.
