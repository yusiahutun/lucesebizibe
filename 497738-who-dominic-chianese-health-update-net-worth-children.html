Dominic Chianese attended the Bronx High School of Science, a public specialised high school in The Bronx in New York City, United States. He graduated in 1948. After high school, Dominic attended Brooklyn College, a public university in Brooklyn, New York.

It took him longer to graduate college because he attended night school to work with his father during the day. Also, he appeared in off-Broadway theatre during his studies. Eventually, he graduated with a Bachelor of Arts in Speech and Theater. Besides college, Chianese attended professional acting classes at HB Studio in Manhattan.
