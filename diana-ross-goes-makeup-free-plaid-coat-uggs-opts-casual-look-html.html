Diana Ross goes makeup free in a plaid coat and UGGs as she opts for the casual look

April 2024 · 3 minute read

Diana Ross went for a very casual California look on Tuesday.

The legendary singer - best known for the hit songs Endless Love and Ain't No Mountain High Enough - wore a plaid coat and UGG boots as she was spotted on at a movie studio in Los Angeles.

And the 74-year-old mother-in-law of Ashlee Simpson also went completely makeup free with her hair down.

The icon was one of the performers lined up to ride the floats through the streets of New York for the 92nd annual Macy's holiday spectacular.

But unlike her peers who did their best to pretend they were singing live, the songstress emphatically mouthed the words completely hands — and care — free.

Despite record low temperatures for the parade, she looked like she was having a ball, smiling and dancing along to her own pre-recorded version of Paul McCartney's Simply Having A Wonderful Christmas Time.

She looked amazing too, clad in a glimmering white gown and enormous fur coat befitting the queen she is.

As for her 'effortless' performance, Twitter could not get enough of it.

The Reach Out And Touch hitmaker was joined at the gig by several members of her family, including daughter Tracee Ellis Ross, son Evan Ross and daughter-in-law Ashlee, who kept warm in a white cashmere scarf and glove set from UGG. 

Like the matriarch, the whole clan dressed entirely in white, although Ashlee's daughter Jagger added a dazzling element in the form of some gold UGG boots.
