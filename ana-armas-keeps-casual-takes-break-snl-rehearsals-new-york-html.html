Ana de Armas keeps it casual as she takes a break from SNL rehearsals in New York

May 2024 · 3 minute read

Ana De Armas kept is casual as she took a break from rehearsals ahead of her Saturday Night Live performance Friday in New York. 

The actress, 34, who is starring in Ghosted with Chris Evans, 41, is slated to host the comedy sketch show for the first time Saturday. 

Taking on the hosting role is something the former Captain America has said he is not brave enough to do. 

I’ve avoided hosting ‘SNL’ like the plague for years now just because I’m so scared. It’s terrifying to me,' the told ET Canada. 

'To me, it would just be one of those things that every single night I would wake up in a cold sweat,' the Avengers star revealed. 

Casual: Ana De Armas kept is casual as she took a break from rehearsals ahead of her Saturday Night Live performance Friday in New York. The actress, 34, is slated to host the comedy sketch show for the first time Saturday

Ana looked comfortable and warm in a pair of straight leg jeans and a puffy black vest over a long sleeve black top. 

The Blonde star wore dark Vans sneakers and square framed sunglasses as she returned from a coffee run. 

She appeared to be wearing little makeup. 

The actress is known for her dramatic roles in films such as Knives Out, No Time to Die and The Gray Man, but now she is trying her hand at comedy. 

One promo for Saturday's SNL episode shows her pitching ideas with series regular Chloe Fineman in which Ana throws out a series of suggestions. 

'I was thinking, "Shh," mixed with, "I’ll break your neck!”' was one as she grabbed the comedian around the throat. 

Colombian artist Karol G will be performing on the show for the first time as well.

Ana has promoted her appearance on the legendary show on social media a little, sharing a series of promos in her Instagram Stories and a photos with post-it notes reading 'Apr 15, Ana De Armas, Karol G.'

First time: Karol G will be joining Ana as musical guest. It will be the Colombian singer's first time appearing on SNL

Social media: Ana has promoted her SNL appearance on Instagram but says she likes to keep a low profile. 'It’s tricky because you feel the pressure to share some personal insight, or something about your private life, to keep people interested in you,' she told Vanity Fair

In an interview with Vanity Fair, the Deep Water revealed she liked to keep her social media presence limited.  'At this point I only have Instagram, and I barely use it because I just feel like things are always wrong on social media,' she claimed.

'If it was up to me, I would delete Instagram right now, but I can’t. I understand that I’m not just an actress. I have other brands that I’m working with and I have other commitments.'

The Cuban-born beauty is a spokesmodel for Estee Lauder and Louis Vuitton. 

'It’s tricky because you feel the pressure to share some personal insight, or something about your private life, to keep people interested in you. You have to find a balance somehow, which I find very difficult.'
