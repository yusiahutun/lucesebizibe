Jilted boyfriend, 27, chased au pair down a street before fatally stabbing her'

A jilted boyfriend chased his au pair girlfriend down a suburban New Jersey street before fatally stabbing her minutes after killing her boss - and then tried to flee the United States using money borrowed from his mom.  

Joseph Porter, 27, of Elizabeth, New Jersey, was booked on two counts of murder and weapons possession in the deaths of his girlfriend, Karen Bermudez-Rodriguez, 26, and her boss David Kimowitz, 40, according to a press release from the Essex County Prosecutor's Office.

On Sunday, neighbors claimed to have seen Porter's parked in the neighborhood for hours before the slayings. 

New details were released on Monday by police in support of murder charges against Porter. In an affidavit, authorities revealed that Bermudez-Rodriguez and Porter had text each other about five hours before the murders occurred. 

She told him that she no longer wanted to be in a relationship, which reportedly 'upset' Porter. 

Police said Bermudez-Rodriguez also told Porter that she wanted her keys to the house back. Bermudez-Rodriguez was a live-in nanny for Kimowitz, his wife, Laura, and their two children.

After his arrest, Porter reportedly admitted to using her key to 'open the door' to Kimowitz's home. 

'Porter then went to the second-floor bedroom where he proceeded to pick up a knife and stab David Kimowitz,' the document reads. 

After stabbing Kimowitz, who is the owner of a comedy club in New York City, Porter bound Bermudez-Rodriguez's hands with tape, but she was able to free herself and run out of the home. 

'Porter then went to the second-floor bedroom where he proceeded to pick up a knife and stab David Kimowitz (pictured with his wife),' the document reads

Porter allegedly chased her down the street and stabbed her multiple times in front of a neighbor's Maplewood home. 

Authorities also said Porter tried to flee the US to Cancun, Mexico, with money from his mother. 

A short time later, a jogger discovered Bermudez-Rodriguez dying of stab wounds on a street outside his residence. 

Prosecutors in Essex County say police officers were called to the home shortly after 6am Saturday on a report of a woman being assaulted. 

Upon arrival they found the injured Bermudez-Rodriguez lying in the street.  

She was rushed to Beth Israel Medical Center where she was pronounced dead shortly before 7.30am, prosecutors said. 

As officers were canvassing the area, Kimowitz was found in a home nearby after police followed a trail of blood to the residence. 

Bermudez-Rodriguez was first stabbed in the home. Kimowitz was pronounced dead at the scene. 

The 40-year-old lived at the home with his wife, Laura, and their two children. His family wasn't at the home, which the couple purchased in 2017, at the time of the killings.
