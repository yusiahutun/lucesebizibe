TikTok Star Caden McGuire Wreck 2023

On February 23, 2023, a serious car accident in Nashville, Tennessee, included TikTok content creator Caden McGuire. At the time of the incident, he was with social media influencer KT Smith. Nonetheless, both of them survived the collision while suffering from several injuries.

Caden informed his followers on Twitter about the accident on 24 February by uploading two parallel images.

In first, he was laying on a hospital bed with cervical traction being applied to his neck and many channels being applied to his torso. He showed a picture of the wrecked SUV’s remains on the second slide. The TikTok celebrity expressed his gratitude for surviving the collision in a post.

Caden was shocked to learn that he was still alive, and he admitted that he was still trying to process it. He expressed his gratitude for everyone’s calls, texts, prayers, and remarks.

The driver at the time of the collision was KT Smith, who is also the ex-fiancee of American country music star Morgan Wallen, according to a post on her Instagram stories. Caden was indicated as being in the passenger seat by KT, who tagged him in her narrative.

KT included the exact image of the totaled vehicle from Caden McGuire’s Twitter post in her initial report. A semi-truck allegedly struck the automobile. As seen by KT’s since-deleted Instagram stories about the event in which she tagged the two, Gabriella Payant appears to have been present as well.

In another report that has since expired, KT retaliated against a user who questioned if drinking alcohol contributed to the accident. She clarified what occurred and why by saying, “some semi ran a red light and demolished us, so thank you for your kindness and concern.”

As for the injuries that she sustained, KT shared several images of her swollen face, writing, “My face is basically broken and I can’t walk on my right side.”

Caden has 2.5 million followers on TikTok. He mostly posts lip-syncs, dance clips, and funny videos.
