The Story Behind Thomas Rhett's Latest Collaboration With Chaco Footwear

September 2024 Â· 4 minute read

Thomas Rhett Atkins had his fans in mind for his latest collaboration, but it was his family and love for the outdoors that was the driving force behind it.

The "Die a Happy Man" singer is once again teaming up with the footwear brand Chaco after last year's successful Boulder collection. Country Living caught up with the singer to hear more about his passion that inspired his latest collaboration, and of course, we had to ask if we're getting the "Side B" to his Country Again: Side A album.

For the love of the great outdoors

Since owning his first pair of Chacos in high school, the shoe has become a staple for Rhett and a mainstay during some of his favorite memories outdoors. Inspired by his love of nature, his latest collection builds on a big passion of his. No, not music. Fishing! "I have really special memories growing up fishing with my dad. My family and I have been obsessed with Chacos since I was a kid, so this is pretty full circle for me," he tells Country Living.

As a hobby that even his daughters have picked up on, it only made sense for Thomas Rhett to dedicate some of his proceeds to foundations that protect the great outdoors for future generations. Fans will be pleased to know that a portion of proceeds for adult shoes go to Trout Unlimited while a portion of the proceeds for kids shoes are dedicated to Love One International. As someone who describes being in nature as "the most peaceful and zen feeling in the world," Rhett's Chaco collaboration emphasizes his belief that "we have a responsibility to protect what gives us so much in return."
