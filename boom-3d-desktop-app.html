Volume Booster

It's not just how the music sounds that changes when using Boom 3D, but also the volume. The app will take the audio levels up a notch to deliver a crisper sound.

3D Surround Sound

To feel fully immersed in whatever it is you're listening to, whether it be in-game music, your favorite tunes, or a movie, 3D surround sound is a must. Boom 3D uses innovative 3D surround sound technology to immersion from every angle.

And, if you're comfortable with making adjustments, you can adjust LFE (subwoofer) gain and the intensity of your audio. Listen to music the way it's meant to be.

Equalizer Presets

The app has many presets you can use for your tracks. Generally, it can automatically detect exactly what preset your track needs, but if you want to meddle and see which one you like best, you can choose between BassBoost, Acoustic, the '60s, Classical, Dubstep, Electronic, Flat, HipHop, House, Jazz, Loud, Music, Party, Pop, Reggae, Rock, Soft, Treble, Vocals, R&B, and Metal.

You can even decide which presets you want to see on the list and which ones you can go without. Take things one step further and arrange the presets in the order you want them to show up in the app. Obviously, your go-to preset should go at the top.

Advanced Audio Player

If you have any downloaded music, Boom 3D will turn into a media player. Otherwise, it will work in the background to improve the sound on your device.

Intensity Slider

When listening to a track, just bring up the Boom 3D app and click on the 3D Surround feature. Once there, you can adjust the 3D audio intensity via a slider, as well as the bass levels. Play around with each slider to see what the optimum level is for you.

Remote Control

When you have Boom 3D installed on your Mac computer, you can install the Boom Remote app on your iOS device. In this way, you'll get playback controls and the ability to adjust the volume levels from your phone even when you're away from your computer. The feature works with Spotify, VLC, QuickTime, and iTunes.

Turn On the Radio

It's not just music from Spotify or Tidal that you can listen to with Boom 3D. You can also go for the radio. The app features access to more than 20,000 local and international radio stations across 120 countries.

You can swap between them in a second, although it will take a bit to connect to each one. When listening to a radio station, you can also fiddle with the tempo, although that's not necessarily something you'll want to do as it will 'ruin' the song.

Boost System FX

Perhaps one of the coolest features you can find on Boom 3D is the Boost System FX. This feature will use the inbuilt audio effects filter to enhance the sound from all the apps.

You can make your own personal adjustments, swapping between equalizer settings, bass, and loudness levels, and you can enable the virtualizer feature.

Basically, you'll get to play around with the Boom 3D features even when watching Netflix, Amazon Prime, YouTube, or Disney Plus, to name a few. If you're used to playing music-based games, those will also get an awesome boost.
