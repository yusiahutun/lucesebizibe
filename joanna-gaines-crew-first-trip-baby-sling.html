Joanna Gaines Wore the Cutest Baby Accessory on Vacation with Son Crew

March 2024 · 2 minute read

Joanna Gaines may be a mom of five with a newborn son, but that's not slowing down her fashion sense.

The Fixer Upper star and Magnolia Market founder was spotted by fans over the weekend visiting Arcadia Farms Cafe in Scottsdale, Arizona, with her husband, Chip, and their 2-month-old son, Crew. Though their family pic of what is likely Crew's first vacation was, of course, adorable, what really caught commenters' eyes was the stylish baby sling the HGTV star was sporting.

Fans were quick to point out that the caramel-covered essential is designed by Wildbird, which seems to have quite the following. "Joanna in her @wildbird is everything," one user wrote. Another said, "Omg omg @wildbird + Chip and Joanna-the best!" This follower summed up everyone's thoughts by writing, "@joannagaines suffice to say the wildbird (and all babywearing) community wants to see a whole lot more of this."

Sadly, Joanna's exact "ring sling" is no longer available, however the fall 2018 collection lands on the brand's site on August 29, meaning you can pick up something similar very soon. Even better: You can steal her low-key, mom-on-the-go look right away, with these styles that start at just $40.
