Tupac Shakur's Baltimore home where he lived as a teenager hits real estate market for $179K

August 2024 · 4 minute read

The Baltimore home where late rap legend Tupac Shakur grew up as a teenager has hit the market for $179,000.

The two-unit dwelling is located on the block of Greenmount Avenue, which is nicknamed Tupac Shakur Way in honor of the late rapper.

The California Love rapper and his relatives initially moved into the apartment on the first floor of the building in 1984 when Shakur was 13-years-old.
