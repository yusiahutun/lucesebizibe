Meet Rosario Dawsons adopted, expectant daughter Isabella: the 44-year-old Ahsoka actress will be

August 2024 · 2 minute read

Rosario Dawson is going to be a grandma – at the youthful age of 44!

The Haunted Mansion actress shared the news during an interview with Page Six in December, saying that her daughter, Isabella, was expecting her first child. “It’s very exciting,” she told the publication, adding that she plans on being called a “Glamma”.

But who is Rosario Dawson’s low-key, 21-year-old daughter? Here’s everything we know.

Isabella Dawson was adopted by Rosario in 2014

According to People, Rosario Dawson adopted a then 11-year-old Isabella in 2014. In an interview with Health in 2021, she said she knew Isabella’s biological mother and when she found out she was in foster care, she reached out to offer her support. “It wasn’t even a question; it was clearly meant to be,” Dawson said, “We are a family, and it’s beautiful.”

Her name is sentimental

The Men In Black 2 actress named her daughter after her grandma. “She’s named after my grandmother, so my grandmother was Isabel. My mom’s Isabel Celeste, I’m Rosario Isabel,” she said on an episode of the We Are Family podcast.

She’s very private

Isabella has remained largely hidden away from the spotlight over the years, though her mother does occasionally bring her to events and posts photos of the pair on social media.

She’s close to her mum

The Mandalorian actress rarely speaks about her daughter in interviews, but when she does, it’s nothing but praise. “I love her so much and she’s such a good person,” she told People. “I love that she’s very girlie. She loves her jewellery and you know, playing dress up.”

Is Rosario Dawson in a relationship?

Dawson is currently dating Nnamdi Okafor, a philanthropic strategist for California State University, per People. She told the publication that they met “at a bar” and that her partner is in “that same space of wanting to be of service”.
