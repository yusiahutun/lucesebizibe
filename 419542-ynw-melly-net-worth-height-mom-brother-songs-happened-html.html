Career

Despite growing up in a rough neighbourhood and harsh conditions, Demons always had a passion for music. So when the rapper was 15 (while still involved with a gang), he started posting music on SoundCloud, bringing him into the limelight.

In 2016, Demons joined the YNW, a hip-hop collective of black rappers. The initials YNW are thought to stand for “Young New Wave” or “Young Nigga World”, according to Demon’s Instagram account. The collective also featured other rappers such as Anthony Williams (YNW Sakchaser), Cortlen Henry (YNW Bortlen), and Christopher Thomas Jr (YNW Juvy).

After joining the YNW collective, Demons adopted the stage name, YNW Melly. He continued working on his music and released his first project in 2017, an extended play dubbed Collect Call. The play contained several songs and featured famous artists such as John Wicks and Lil B.
