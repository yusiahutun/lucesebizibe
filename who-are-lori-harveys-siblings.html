Who Are Lori Harveys Siblings? A Closer Look at the Harvey Family

July 2024 · 7 minute read

Lori Harvey might be the most famous daughter of Steve Harvey, but the model has six other siblings who are also thriving.

Lori Harvey is a name that denotes a lot of things in the entertainment industry. She is one of the hottest models in Hollywood, and her reputation of dating some of the biggest stars also often gets her in the news.

Lori is also an accomplished model, although she is famously known as the daughter of renowned comedian Steve Harvey. As a young child, Lori had dreams of being an equestrian. In addition, she wanted to one day represent the United States at the Olympics. 

Lori rode horses throughout high school and continued after she enrolled at a college in Florida. Sadly, her dreams got cut short after suffering a back injury that ended her riding career.

Lori dropped out of college and started taking online classes. She has worked with famous brands like Dolce and Gabbana and appeared on runways during Paris and Milan fashion weeks.

Although Lori might be the most famous child of Steve Harvey, the comedian has six other children.  Before his marriage to Marjorie, Steve already had four kids from previous marriages. Also, Marjorie was a mother to three kids from her relationships.

After their marriage in 2007, Steve adopted Lori and her two siblings. In addition to his four kids, the comedian became a father of seven.

Lori Harvey’s Siblings

Lori Harvey has three older sisters, two older brothers, and a younger brother.  

Broderick Harvey Jr 

Broderick Harvey Jr is Lori’s eldest brother. On August 21, 1991, he was born to Steve Harvey and his first wife, Marcia Harvey.  Broderick attended high school at North Springs Charter High School in Sandy Springs, Georgia.

In 2010 he enrolled at Morehouse College in Atlanta, Georgia, and graduated in 2014 with a degree in Arts.  Broderick did not have a close relationship with his father Steve until he was 16, when he started living with him.

The pair have since become very close and even worked on various projects together. In 2008 Broderick started working with Steve on the presenter’s radio network’s social media team. 

After a while, he started his own photography company called “B. Harvey Photography Inc.” In 2015 he worked as a ticket coordinator for “NuOpp Inc” and partnered with his friend Thabiti Stephens to start a business called “Steps by Stephens.” He also appeared with his siblings on “Celebrity Family Feud,” hosted by their father. Broderick is also currently single.

Wynton Harvey

Wynton Harvey is Steve’s youngest son. On July 18, 1997, he was born to the comedian and his second wife, Mary Lee Harvey. He was only eight years old when his parents went their separate ways. 

After their widely publicized divorce, Steve gained custody of Wynton. He started living with his father and Marjorie at just ten years old. He also grew very close to his stepmother and his step-siblings.

He attended a local hometown school to obtain his high school degree before enrolling at Florida International University in 2015. Wynton was at the school till 2017 before enrolling at Savannah College of Art and Design in Atlanta, Georgia.

Wynton developed an interest in photography quite early in life and has worked with top brands like Saint Laurent and artists like 2 Chainz and Bosco. The artist also showcases his works on his Instagram account.

He also occasionally works as a fashion model. Wynton is currently in a relationship with Taylor Gordan. The couple first met in 2016, and they have been inseparable since then.

Jason Harvey

Jason Harvey is Lori’s second oldest brother. On August 27, 1991, he was born to Marjorie Harvey and her husband, Jim Townsend. He was only a year old when his biological father, Jim, was arrested for running an illegal drug business.

Steve Harvey adopted him in 2105 after marrying Marjorie, and Jason was also able to take his last name. Although he never really knew his biological father, Jason became quite close with Steve.

While appearing on the Steve Harvey show, Jason praised his stepfather for the role he played in his life. Jason declared:

“You took my mother, but you gained a lot more with it. As a man, you gave me your last name, and it is not something many people do. My father, he did what he could, but he was not there, but you stepped in, and you gave me that.”

Jason is currently married to his wife, model Amanda Hill. The pair have been married for seven years and are parents to four kids; their first child, daughter Rose Harvey, was born on June 19, 2014.

They welcomed their second child son Noah on August 24, 2015. The third child Ezra was born on September 5, 2018. Joey Iris is the couple’s fourth child and second daughter, born on July 2, 2020.

Jason works as a creative director, producer, designer, and artist. He co-owns a luxury footwear company called “Yevrah” with his business partner Erwin Sweetwine.

Brandi Harvey

Brandi is one of Steve’s twins whom he welcomed with his first wife, Marcia. She was born alongside her twin Karli on August 20, 1982. She was just 12 years old when her parents broke up in 1994. However, their separation did not hinder her, and she grew successfully. Brandi spent most of her childhood among her relatives in Atlanta, Georgia.

Brandi enrolled at the Ohio state university in 2002 and graduated with a degree in Arts in 2005. She has worked with different organizations, including Young, Fit and Fly and Steve and Marjorie Harvey Foundation. Brandi is also a wellness coach and public speaker.

She is the author of “ Breakthrough Sold Separately.” Brandi is also the founder of “Beyond Her” an active wellness brand for women of color. Brandi is not married, and she keeps her romantic relationships very private.

Karli Harvey

Karli is the other twin and was born alongside Brandi on August 20. 1982. Like her twin sister, she was also 12 years old when her parents divorced. Afterward, mother Marcia raised Karli, Brandi, and Broderick.

She is a public speaker and TV star, having appeared on “Black Love” and “Steve Harvey.” Karli was not initially in good relations with her father, Steve, because he left them for a while after divorcing their mother.

However, they reconciled, and Steve even walked her down the aisle when she married her husband Benjamin Raymond in 2015. She and Ben became parents in 2016 when they welcomed their son, Troy. 

Morgan Harvey

Morgan Harvey is Marjorie’s daughter from her relationship with Darnell Woods. She was born on June 1, 1985. After graduating from high school, she attended Spelman College in Atlanta.

Morgan was very interested in cooking, and she decided to follow her passion. She enrolled at the Culinary Institute of America in New York. In 2011 she graduated with a degree in Baking and Pastry.

She subsequently became a food blogger, and her blog “I need some Mo” is where she shares food recipes. Morgan has also authored a cookbook titled “Party Heart.”. In addition, she occasionally appeared on Steve’s shows to teach people how to cook. 

Morgan is currently married to her husband, Kareem Vincent-Patrick Hawthorne. They met as university students in 2006 but only started dating after graduating. The pair got married on October 12, 2013. The couple has since become parents to two daughters, Elle Monroe and Marley Jean.

Lori And Her Siblings At Steve’s Walk Of Fame

Lori Harvey and the rest of her six siblings were present to support their father as the famous comedian and presenter received a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame. 

During the event, Steve reminisced about his start in the industry, and his success was a miracle. He also thanked his kids for sacrificing their time with him so he could become successful. 

Steve also gave a special shout-out to his wife Marjorie and disclosed that she saved his life. Steve’s celebrity friends also congratulated him, including Ellen DeGeneres.
