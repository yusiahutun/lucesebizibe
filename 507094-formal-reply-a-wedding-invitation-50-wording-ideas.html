Formal reply to a wedding invitation: 50+ best wording ideas

June 2024 · 8 minute read

Receiving an invitation to any event triggers the best feeling. Therefore, it is responsible to respond appropriately. Unfortunately, not everyone can give a good formal reply to a wedding invitation. That is why the best wording ideas would come in handy.

Formal reply to a wedding invitation ideas

A formal invitation, such as for a wedding, requires a response that is formal and cordial at the same time. These are the best ways to formally reply to a wedding invite.

  • So happy to get this in the post this morning. What a beautiful invitation from two beautiful people. I can’t wait to celebrate this special milestone with you.
  • Dear Vivian and Joel, Congratulations on this exciting milestone! We always knew you two were meant to be. I can’t wait to see you make it official. We will absolutely be there. Love, your biggest supporters, Frederick and Ruth.
  • I am super excited to attend your wedding and celebrate the day. Thank you for inviting our whole family! We’re looking forward to it!
  • I am so pleased to accept your kind invitation to the wedding on April 1st this year. I wish you every happiness and look forward to witnessing the first day of the rest of your lives.
  • Emmy and I are so happy for you and cannot wait to be a part of the festivities. Best wishes, Peterson and Georgina Oppenheim.
  • I received your beautiful invitation today. And I am delighted that you have considered me important to be a part of your great moment. I appreciate the invitation so much!
  • Mr and Mrs [Name] accept with pleasure the kind invitation of Mr and Mrs [Name] to the wedding reception of their daughter [Name] and Mr [Name] on Sunday, March 17th, at 2:00 PM.
  • Our two favourite people are getting hitched! And, of course, we’ll be there. So happy for you and can’t wait to have an absolute blast on August 10th—hugs from all of us.
  • Mr and Mrs [Name] accept with pleasure your kind wedding invitation for Saturday, July 7th. We are looking forward to the celebration.

How to respond to a wedding RSVP

RSVP is the French word and means “reply if it pleases you.” And most of the wedding invitations will include a deadline for the RSVP. A formal reply to a wedding invitation would come in handy. Here is what you need to know while you politely respond to an invitation.

  • Check the invitation for RSVP instructions.
  • Create a new email draft and carefully type in the email address of the host.
  • Respond as soon as possible
  • Keep your response short and sweet.
