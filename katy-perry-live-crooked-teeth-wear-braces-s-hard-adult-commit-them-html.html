Katy Perry would rather live with crooked teeth than wear braces

April 2024 Â· 3 minute read

The stunning pop princess is flawless in every way.

But when it comes to her teeth, Katy Perry thinks her bottom row of gnashers are her biggest beauty flaw. 

The 31-year-old won't get her crooked lower teeth fixed because she won't 'commit' to having braces.

No brace face! Katy Perry, 31, revealed in the latest issue of InStyle magazine that she won't get her bottom teeth fixed because she doesn't want braces

Metal mouth! The pop princess as a braced-faced teen from her music video in Last Friday Night (T.G.I.F.) from her third studio album, Teenage Dream

Speaking to the latest issue of InStyle magazine she said, 'I want my lower teeth to be perfect. I never had braces and it's hard as an adult to commit to them.' 

The Roar hit-maker is reluctant to get them fixed because she doesn't want to live with a dental straightener as an adult. 

'Those teeth are the bane of my beauty existence. It's like the Rocky Mountains in there!'

Although she's not keen on fiddling with her teeth, the sexy pop star has always been happy to experiment with her hair colour and clothes.
