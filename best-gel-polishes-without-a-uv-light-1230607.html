Gel nail polishes are thick and highly pigmented forms of nail lacquers that usually require curing under UV LED lamps to offer a more durable, and chip-resistant color. Using these recommendations, you can achieve professional-grade nails without spending too much on a UV lamp or on salon visits. 

Gel nail polishes are currently stirring up buzz in the professional manicure industry.     How to Select the Best Gel Nail Polish Without a UV Light?

    To choose the best nail gel nail polish that does not require UV lamps for curing, you have to keep certain factors in mind, such as:

    1. Ingredients: Gel nail polishes are made with various chemicals that provide them their unique properties like vibrant color, adhesion, durability, etc. Therefore it is essential to avoid products with ingredients that may not be suitable for sensitive skin, or ones that may contain potential allergens or skin-irritants in the formula. To make your search process easier, pay attention to indications labeled as "3-free" or "5-free,"  which means those formulations are devoid of harmful ingredients like formaldehyde, toluene, DBP, camphor, or formaldehyde resin.

    2. Quality of the Formula: The formulations of gel nail polishes are usually made to be durable, chip-resistant, and super-pigmented. Therefore, look out for such high-grade formulations that claim to have these features. They should have great reviews too. Additionally, you can also check for the consistency of the formula such as gel polishes with smooth consistencies that are easy to spread evenly and do not require additional curing or prepping time. This prevents you from spending a lot of time, energy, or money. 

    3. Budget: It is essential to fix a budget and conduct your own market study by comparing the prices of different gel polishes available online. While creating a budget for yourself, you must also consider that although there are several gel nail polishes available at lower prices, usually products that offer higher quality, performance, and longevity have a slightly higher cost.

    FAQ's

    Is there a gel nail polish that doesn’t need UV light?

    Yes, there are gel nail polishes that will give equally vibrant, glossy, and long-lasting colors without a UV lamp. You can check our recommendations mentioned above to choose the perfect one for yourself.

    Do all gel polishes require UV light?

    No, not all gel nail polishes require UV light to be cured and perfected, some examples are mentioned in this article.

    How do you dry gel nails without a UV LED lamp?

    You may air dry your gel nail polish, or you may immerse your fingers in an ice bath for quicker results.

    Can you use gel nail polish without UV light?

    Yes you can, however, the drying time is going to be too long which sometimes might even take days to set completely. So, other than gel nail polishes that are specially made to be used without UV lamps, curing them under UV light is usually a convenient option.
