5 Best Latte Machines to Get Your Starbucks Fix at Home

September 2024 · 5 minute read

If there is one thing the quarantine has taught us (besides that we’re capable of baking really delicious bread and dry shampoo is amazing), it’s that the Starbucks addiction we once joked about was absolutely real. With many coffee shops across the country closed due to social distancing requirements, we’re left to fend for ourselves and find new ways to get our caffeine fix. Luckily, a simple-to-use latte machine makes it easy to recreate your favorite coffee drinks at home. 

What Machine Makes the Best Latte?

Whether you’re trying a new coffee-based diet or have an appreciation for finer brews, the best latte machine will allow you to create Barista-quality coffee from the comfort of your home. Depending on your preferences, a reliable machine will provide you with the option to customize your temperature, milk foam levels, and coffee strength without the added accessories.

What is The Best Machine For At-Home Use?

While taste is a big factor to consider, you’ll also want to take into consideration your kitchen space. For larger areas you can get away with machine that’s more bulky, but if you’re short on counter space, then consider a more compact design.

Below are five espresso machines that will turn you into a master barista with the press of a button. And when the pandemic is over, you’ll be free of your Starbucks addiction and will save a ton of money making your own grande skim caramel macchiato from scratch.

Check out our handy guide for how to make a latte at home once you’ve snagged your dream espresso machine!

Keurig K-Cafe Coffee Maker

Best combo latte machine

Buy on Amazon ($169.99, originally $199.99)

Why we like it:

  • Similar to regular Keurig machine
  • Comes with dishwasher safe milk frother
  • Customizable foam

If you’re a K-Cup lover but want barista-style lattes, this is the machine for you. Just as you do with a regular Keurig, simply pop in a K-cup and use the button-controls to customize your brew. What sets this apart from other pod machines is its dishwasher-safe milk frother. Fill it with milk and select the type of foam you crave (settings are for cold brew, cappuccino, and latte). Then add it to your coffee and enjoy!

One Amazon reviewer wrote, “The frother is astonishing! I’m still not entirely sure how that little booger works: but within a minute, half & half in a room temperature frothing mug [becomes] the consistency of shaving cream. Just fill it to the appropriate line, put the lid on and hit the button. Done.”

De’Longhi Nespresso

Best Single Serve Latte Machine

Buy on Amazon ($379.95)

Why we like it:

  • Has a built-in milk frother
  • Added pump extracts all the flavors
  • Prevents a watered-down brew

If you prefer lattes, espressos, and cappuccinos to coffee, this is your machine. The built-in milk frother adds steamed milk to the coffee as it brews for the perfect, single-cup barista blend. The high-performance pump extracts all the flavors, so you won’t have to worry about a watered-down brew.

One Amazon reviewer wrote, “It takes 25 seconds to have a wonderful espresso and about 35 seconds to have a cappuccino. What a fantastic world! Small appliance that doesn’t take up lots of counter space and looks elegant.”
