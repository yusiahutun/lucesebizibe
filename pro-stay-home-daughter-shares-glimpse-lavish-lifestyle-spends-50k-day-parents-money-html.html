Pro stay-at-home daughter shares glimpse into lavish lifestyle, spends $50K a day of parents' money

September 2024 · 8 minute read

A 'professional stay-at-home daughter' has become a viral TikTok star after sharing a glimpse into her lavish lifestyle - which involves spending more than $50,000 a day (of her parents' money) on things like blowouts, designer clothes, Pilates classes, and Botox.

Roma Abdesselam, 26, from New York, often flaunts her luxurious life on TikTok, where she has racked up hundreds of thousands of followers, who are often left stunned by her steep spending habits.

Roma - who hasn't worked in more than two years - forks out thousands of dollars a day on luxury goods from brands like Gucci, Dior, Chanel, and Prada, all of which she puts on her mom and dad's credit cards.

Roma's days usually involve sleeping past noon, going on shopping sprees, getting her hair done, working out, receiving massages, and dining at expensive NYC eateries - which she often documents and shares to her 629,000 TikTok followers.

'I’ve turned spending [my parents’] money as a stay-at-home daughter into a job, and that’s been very, very fun for me,' she told the New York Post recently.

'Every day I wake up in the morning, have breakfast, go to a [$600] workout class and go shopping with my girlfriends at Bergdorf’s, and that’s my life being unemployed.'

Roma, who hasn't disclosed what her parents do for work, told the outlet that she was planning to launch her own skincare line before the pandemic hit, but put it on hold amid COVID-19. However, she is now making an income by sharing sponsored posts and advertisements to her social media accounts and wants to focus on growing her online presence. 

In one viral video, Roma showed what a 'day in the life of a professional stay-at-home daughter' is really like, beginning with a trip to the beauty salon, which cost her $600.

In the video, the 26-year-old explained that she used to have 'big bushy' eyebrows but she 'over-tweezed them,' so she had to get them fixed.

She then went to a bar before going shopping because 'the best way to shop is obviously drunk,' she said. 

Roma spent $1,353 on various perfumes and skincare products from Cos Bar before heading to Zimmerman where she dropped $2,221. She then bought some lingerie from Agent Provocateur for $2,774 and spent another $2,264 at Intermix. 

She finished her day with an $100 private Pilates session, bringing her total to $9,326 for the day - which was low for her.
