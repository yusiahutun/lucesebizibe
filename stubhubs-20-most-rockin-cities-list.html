StubHub's '20 Most Rockin' Cities' List

June 2024 · 2 minute read

San Francisco, CA (CelebrityAccess MediaWire) — secondary ticket website StubHub, today announced its "20 Most Rockin' Cities" for 2007 based on overall gross ticket sales in each market. StubHub has compiled its second annual list of America's "20 Most Rockin' Cities." Summer concerts in these top 20 cities have been the most active sellers on StubHub with rankings based on total sales for concert events only, occurring between May 15th and September 15th.

Additionally, StubHub has included the three best selling shows in each city. New York, Los Angeles and Chicago held their distinctions again this year as the top three Most Rockin' Cities. The Police tour was the top grossing and most popular act in more than half of the top 20 cities.

America's 20 Most Rockin' Cities:

2007's Top Selling Shows:

1. New York Metro The Police – 8/3, 8/1, 8/5
2. Los Angeles The Police – 6/23, 6/20, 6/21
3. Chicago – The Police – 7/6, 7/5, Eric Clapton
4. Boston – The Police – 7/28, 7/29, Kenny Chesney
5. Philadelphia – Jimmy Buffett, Kenny Chesney, The Police 7/19
6. San Francisco Bay Area – The Police, Rock the Bells, Eric Clapton

7. Dallas – The Police – 6/26, 6/27, Jimmy Buffett
8. Las Vegas – The Police, Dave Matthews Band – 3/23, 3/24

9. Detroit – Kenny Chesney, The Police, Billy Joel
10. Hartford – The Police, Dave Mathews Band, Elton John
11. Miami – The Police, Billy Joel, Justin Timberlake
12. Atlanta – Dave Matthews, The Police, Soul 2 Soul
13. Denver – The Police, Eric Clapton, Rush
14. Phoenix – The Police, Keith Urban, Eric Clapton
15. Washington DC Metro – Red Hot Chili Peppers, Soul 2 Soul, Keith Urban
16. Seattle – Kenny Chesney, The Police – 6/6, 6/7

17. Richmond, VA. – Jimmy Buffett, Dave Matthews Band, Kenny Chesney
18. Houston – The Police, Jimmy Buffett, Eric Clapton

19. Atlantic City, NJ – Jimmy Buffett, Tool, The Police
20. Tampa, FL – The Police, Kenny Chesney, Soul 2 Soul
