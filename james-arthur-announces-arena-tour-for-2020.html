James Arthur Announces Arena Tour For 2020

July 2024 · 2 minute read

(CelebrityAccess) — “Say You Won’t Let Go” recording artist James Arthur announced he’s lined up an arena tour of the UK and the US which kicks off early next year.

Arthur’s ‘The YOU Tour’ American dates start on April 4th at the Egyptian Room at Old National Centre in Indianapolis, Ind. and wrap on May 24th at The Van Buren in Phoenix, AZ.

Arthur is touring in support of his latest full-length album YOU, which he released on Oct. 18th via Columbia Records.

He was recently awarded a disc for reaching a billion streams on Spotify and a billion views on YouTube with his No.1 hit “Say You Won’t Let Go.” Only 18 records have achieved a billion streams on Spotify in history, and James is one of 10 artists globally to do this including Drake, Ed Sheeran and Justin Bieber.
