We’ve all heard of Kim Kardashian. Most of us know about her glamorous lifestyle and, let’s be real, envy her for it. That’s why we LOVE the Kim Kardashian: Hollywood game.

In the Kim Kardashian: Hollywood game, you can create your very own character that looks like you AND is an aspiring celebrity. The whole aim of the game is to level up and rise to fame, by completing tasks that come your way and of course, being absolutely fabulous.

The game was created by Glu Games Inc. for IOS, Android, Mac and Facebook. So there is no excuse for not living your very own red carpet adventure yet.

Where is Chateau Nuit in Kim Kardashian’s game?

If there is something we can all agree on about the game is that you would have to have superpowers to remember every single location and how to get there. For a normal human being, it’s just impossible.

Chateau Nuit in Screenshot of Youtube video “Kim Kardashian: Hollywood – Locations for Gigs and Appearances“

Chateau Nuit is a discotheque – more commonly known as a club – where you can call contacts for a date or meet new people. You might also have an appearance here. Overall, it’s a really well-connected social location.

However, Chateau Nuit is also one of those locations that you could spend hours looking for, without the right clues. Here is the insider scoop on where to find it.

  • SEE ALSO: Keeping Up with the Kardashians season 15 recap

How to find Chateau Nuit

Chateau Night is located in Las Vegas, which is the 5th location you unlock as you level up in the game. In order to get there, you need to go to LAX and take a plane to Las Vegas, which will cost you twenty bucks. Once you are in Las Vegas, there are only three buildings. Chateau Nuit is the one at the end of the street.

Now that you have found your way, get ready to be surrounded by famous actors and entertainers. If you’re lucky, you might even spot some royals from countries no one in the game has ever heard of.

Pro tip: Chateau Nuit is the perfect place to throw a birthday party. You’re welcome.

Now game away and let your inner celeb shine!
